## Overview

Catan Junior is a simplified version of Settlers of Catan with a pirate theme. Players build pirates' lairs and ships on a fixed island board, collecting resources when islands produce goods. A Ghost Captain blocks production, and Coco the parrot provides helpful bonuses. The first player to build all 7 of their pirates' lairs wins.

## Components

- 1 double-sided game board (3-4 player side / 2-player side)
- 28 pirates' lairs (7 per color in 4 colors)
- 32 ships (8 per color in 4 colors)
- 1 Ghost Captain figure
- 1 die
- Resource tiles (cutlasses, goats, wood, gold, molasses)
- Coco tiles (parrot bonus tiles)
- 4 building costs tiles (1 per player)

## Setup

1. Place the board (3-4 player side for 3-4 players; 2-player side for 2 players).
2. Place the Ghost Captain on Spooky Island.
3. Each player chooses a color and takes 7 pirates' lairs and 8 ships.
4. Place 1 pirate's lair on each of your 2 color-coded starting lair-sites and 1 ship on each of your 2 starting ship-sites.
5. Keep remaining 5 lairs and 6 ships as your supply.
6. Sort resource tiles into 5 types as stockpile.
7. Place 1 of each resource tile on the 5 marketplace booths.
8. Shuffle Coco tiles and place face-down in a stack.
9. Each player takes their building costs tile and receives 1 wood + 1 molasses tile.

## Turn Structure

Youngest player goes first; play proceeds clockwise.

**1. Roll the Die:**
- All islands showing the rolled number produce resources.
- Every player with a pirate's lair adjacent to a producing island receives 1 matching resource tile per lair.
- If you have 2 lairs next to the same island, you get 2 resources.

**2. Roll a 6 — Ghost Captain:**
- No resources are produced.
- Move the Ghost Captain to any island of your choice.
- Take 2 resource tiles of that island's type from the stockpile.
- That island produces nothing for anyone while the Ghost Captain is there.

**3. Build and Buy (active player only):**
- **Pirates' lair:** 1 cutlass + 1 molasses + 1 goat + 1 wood. Must be on an empty lair-site adjacent to one of your ships.
- **Ship:** 1 goat + 1 wood. Must be on an empty ship-site adjacent to one of your lairs.
- **Coco tile:** 1 cutlass + 1 molasses + 1 gold. Flip and resolve immediately.
- Build/buy as many times as resources allow.

**4. Trade (active player only):**
- **Marketplace (once per turn):** Take any 1 resource from the marketplace; place 1 of your resources on that same booth.
- **Stockpile (unlimited):** Trade 2 identical resource tiles to the stockpile for any 1 resource tile.

## Actions

**Coco tile effects (resolve immediately when purchased):**
- Move the Ghost Captain (as if you rolled a 6)
- Build a pirates' lair or ship for free
- Receive the 4 resources shown from the stockpile

**Spooky Island lair:** The player with the most Coco tiles places a lair on Spooky Island (this counts toward the 7 needed to win). If another player ties or surpasses your Coco count, you remove the lair; the new leader places theirs instead.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to build all 7 pirates' lairs wins immediately. A lair on Spooky Island counts as one of the 7.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- You must build in alternating fashion: lair then ship, or ship then lair. You cannot build past another player's lair or ship, and you cannot share lair-sites or ship-sites.
- **Marketplace reset:** If 5 tiles of the same type accumulate in the marketplace, remove all marketplace tiles and refill with 1 of each type.
- **Resource shortage:** If the stockpile runs out of a resource type, all players return all of their tiles of that type to the stockpile. That resource becomes available on the next turn.
- The board has no "6" printed on any island (rolling 6 always triggers the Ghost Captain).
- **Advanced rule (optional):** Players may trade resources directly with each other on the active player's turn.
