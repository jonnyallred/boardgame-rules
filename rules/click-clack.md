## Overview

Click Clack is a children's dexterity/magnetic game published by Hasbro. Players use a magnetic wand to hunt for magnetic snack tokens scattered across the play area while trying to outsmart Freddy the Piglet. The game involves quick reflexes and dexterity as players race to collect the most snacks using their magnetic wands.

## Components

- Magnetic wand(s)
- Magnetic snack tokens (scattered across play area)
- Freddy the Piglet figure/mechanism
- Game base or play area

## Setup

1. Scatter the magnetic snack tokens across the play area.
2. Place Freddy the Piglet in his starting position.
3. Each player takes a magnetic wand.
4. Determine a starting player.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Use your magnetic wand** to try to pick up snack tokens from the play area.
2. **Avoid or interact with Freddy** according to the game's mechanism.
3. **Collect snacks** successfully attracted to your wand.

## Actions

### Collect Snacks
- Wave or touch your magnetic wand near the scattered snack tokens.
- Tokens that stick to the wand are successfully collected.
- You have a limited time or number of attempts per turn.

### Freddy the Piglet
- Freddy acts as an obstacle or opponent.
- If Freddy interacts with your collection attempt (details vary by edition), you may lose collected snacks or your turn.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- Each collected snack token is worth points.
- The game ends when all snack tokens have been collected or a set number of rounds is completed.
- The player with the most snack tokens wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- The game is designed for young children (ages 4+) and emphasizes motor skills and fun over strategy.
- Magnetic interference can cause unexpected token movement, adding to the excitement.
- Quick play time makes it suitable for multiple rounds.
- Different editions may have slightly different mechanisms for Freddy's interference.
