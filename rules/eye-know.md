## Overview

Eye Know is a visual trivia game where players identify images on cards and answer trivia questions about them. The game offers two modes: a basic version where the first player to collect 5 cards wins, and a classic version with betting chips where the player with the most chips after 5 rounds wins.

## Components

- 400 Game cards (each with an image on the front and three trivia questions on the back)
- 2 Six-sided dice (1 color die, 1 number die)
- Game board with a 4x3 card grid and draw pile space

## Setup

### Basic Mode
1. Deal 12 cards from one deck face-up onto the game board in a 4x3 grid.
2. Place extra cards on the draw pile space.
3. Choose a first player.

### Classic Mode (With Betting Chips)
1. Arrange 12 cards in a 4x3 grid on the board.
2. Place extra cards on the draw pile space.
3. Give each player 5 betting chips (supply your own chips or keep score on paper).
4. Choose a first player.

## Turn Structure

### Basic Mode
1. **Roll the dice** — Color die determines which card color to choose; number die determines the question type (1 = True/False, 2 = Multiple Choice, 3 = Open-Ended).
2. **Identify an image** — Point to a card matching the die color and guess what is shown. The player to your right confirms. If incorrect, turn ends; card is discarded and replaced.
3. **Answer a trivia question** — If you identified the image, the player to your right reads the question matching the number die. Correct: keep the card. Incorrect: discard the card. Either way, draw a replacement card.

### Classic Mode
1. **Identify an image** — Choose any of the 12 images; no dice needed. Point and guess. If correct, receive 1 chip and proceed to the trivia question. If incorrect, turn ends; card is discarded.
2. **Place a bet** — Bet 1-5 chips.
3. **Choose question difficulty** and answer:
   - True/False, Multiple Choice, or Open-Ended
4. Correct: get your bet back plus payout per the chart. Incorrect: lose your bet.
5. Whether correct or incorrect, keep the identified card. Draw a replacement.

## Actions

- **Identify** an image by pointing and naming it
- **Answer** a trivia question at the chosen difficulty
- **Bet** chips (Classic mode only)

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

### Basic Mode
First player to collect **5 cards** wins.

### Classic Mode
After each player has had **5 turns**, cash in card color sets for bonus chips:

| Set Type | Bonus |
|----------|-------|
| 3 cards of the same color | 5 chips |
| 4 cards of the same color | 10 chips |
| 1 card from each color | 15 chips |
| 5 cards of the same color | 20 chips |

No card may be used in more than one set. The player with the most chips wins.

### Betting Payouts (Classic Mode)

| Your Bet | True/False Pays | Multiple Choice Pays | Open-Ended Pays |
|----------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 chip | 2 chips | 2 chips | 3 chips |
| 2 chips | 4 chips | 4 chips | 6 chips |
| 3 chips | 6 chips | 6 chips | 9 chips |
| 4 chips | 8 chips | 8 chips | 12 chips |
| 5 chips | 10 chips | 10 chips | 15 chips |

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Younger players variant:** Skip dice; allow younger players to select any image and answer any question type.
- **Betting chips not included:** You must supply your own or track scores on paper.
- **Card replacement:** Always replace discarded or won cards immediately from the draw pile.
- **Each card has a description hint** at the bottom of the image to help with identification.
