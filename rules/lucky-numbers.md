## Overview

Lucky Numbers is a light tile-placement game where each player tries to fill their 4x4 garden grid with numbered clovers. The catch: numbers in each row must be in ascending order left to right, and in each column ascending order top to bottom. The first player to completely fill their board wins.

## Components

- 4 sets of 20 Clover tiles in 4 colours, numbered 1-20 each (80 tiles total)
- 4 Game boards (4x4 grids)

## Setup

1. Use one complete set of Clover tiles (1 colour, numbered 1-20) per player. With fewer than 4 players, return unused sets to the box.
2. Shuffle all clovers and place them face-down in the middle of the table. (Colours have no significance during play — they are only for setup counting.)
3. Each player takes a game board, oriented with the ladybug in the lower-right corner.
4. Each player draws 4 clovers and places them face-up on the four diagonal spaces (marked with golden edges).

**Variant (Michael's Setup):** Players draw clovers one at a time, placing each on any remaining diagonal space, without relocating previously placed tiles.

## Turn Structure

The oldest player begins; play proceeds clockwise.

On your turn, choose one of two options:

### Option A: Take a Face-Down Clover
- Draw a face-down clover from the centre.
- Place it face-up on your board following the Placement Rules.
- If you cannot or do not want to place it, leave it face-up in the centre.

### Option B: Take a Face-Up Clover
- Take one face-up clover from the centre.
- Place it on your board following the Placement Rules.
- This option is only available if there is at least one face-up clover in the centre.
- Do not draw an additional clover.

## Actions

### Placement Rules
- You may place the clover on any **empty** space on your board, OR
- **Exchange** it with a previously placed clover (return the exchanged clover face-up to the centre).
- The number must fit in **ascending order** with all other numbers in its row (left to right) and column (top to bottom).
- Numbers in a row/column do not need to be consecutive (e.g., 3, 7, 15 is valid).
- Identical numbers in the same row or column are **not allowed**.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to fill every space on their 4x4 board in accordance with the Placement Rules wins.

**Rare edge case:** If the last face-down clover is drawn and no player has completed their board, the player with the fewest empty spaces wins. Ties result in multiple winners.

### Tournament Mode (Variant)
Play as many games as there are players (so each player is start player once).
- Winner scores **2 points**.
- Other players lose **1 point per empty space** on their board.
- Player with the most points (or fewest negative) after all games wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Once placed during setup, diagonal clovers cannot be relocated.
- During gameplay, exchanging a tile is optional — you may always place on an empty space instead.
- If you draw a face-down tile and cannot legally place it anywhere, you must leave it face-up in the centre.
- Low numbers should generally go upper-left; high numbers lower-right.

## Player Reference

**Board Layout (4x4 grid):**
- Rows: ascending left to right
- Columns: ascending top to bottom
- Numbers range: 1-20

**Turn Options:**
| Option | Action |
|--------|--------|
| A | Draw face-down clover; place or discard face-up |
| B | Take face-up clover; must place it |

**Win Condition:** Fill all 16 spaces with valid ascending order in every row and column.
