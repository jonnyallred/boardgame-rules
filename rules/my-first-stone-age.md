# My First Stone Age

## Overview

My First Stone Age is a family-friendly set collection and memory game where players are young villagers in prehistoric times. Players flip forest tokens to determine their actions -- collecting resources, trading at the market, or moving along a path. The first player to build 3 huts by collecting the right combinations of resources wins.

## Components

- 1 game board (circular path with locations)
- 4 player figures
- 14 forest tokens (face-down with various symbols)
- Resource tokens (berries, fish, pots, arrowheads, teeth)
- Hut tiles
- 1 dog figure
- Building spots

## Setup

1. Place the board in the center. Each player places their figure on the starting village.
2. Shuffle the 14 forest tokens and place them face down around the board.
3. Sort resources and place them in the supply.
4. Lay out hut tiles in the building area, each showing required resource combinations.
5. The youngest player goes first.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Flip a forest token** to reveal its symbol.
2. **Perform the action** shown on the token.
3. **Turn the token back face down.**

## Actions

| Token Symbol | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| **Resource** (berry, fish, pot, arrowhead, tooth) | Collect 1 matching resource from the supply |
| **Dog** | Move the dog to any forest token and peek at it (remember it!) |
| **Dice** | Roll the die and move your figure that many spaces forward on the path; perform the action at the location |
| **Exchange** | Trade any 2 resources for 1 of your choice |
| **Wild** | Collect any 1 resource of your choice |

### Locations on the Path

When your figure stops at a location:
- **Forest**: Flip a forest token.
- **Trading Post**: Exchange resources.
- **Building Site**: Build a hut if you have the right resources.

### Building Huts

When you have the exact combination of resources shown on a hut tile, you may build it. Return the resources to the supply and take the hut tile.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to build **3 huts** wins the game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Forest tokens are always turned face down after use; remembering their positions is key.
- You can only build 1 hut per turn.
- Resources are unlimited in the supply.
- The dog token is a memory aid -- you can look at any face-down forest token when you flip the dog.

## Player Reference

| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Flip | Turn over 1 forest token |
| Act | Collect resource, move, trade, or use special action |
| Build | Trade matching resources for a hut tile |
| Win | First to build 3 huts |
