## Overview

Dance Arena 200 is a team-based party game revolving around dance and physical activity. Players form teams and compete through various dance-related challenges, including naming dances, performing moves, teaching steps, and identifying dance styles. The game uses a sand timer for timed rounds and combines physical activity with trivia knowledge about dance.

## Components

- Challenge cards (dance-related activities and trivia)
- Sand timer
- Game board or scoring track
- Player/team markers
- Rules booklet

## Setup

1. Divide players into teams.
2. Place the game board or scoring track in the center.
3. Shuffle challenge cards and place them in a draw pile.
4. Set the sand timer nearby.
5. Determine which team goes first.

## Turn Structure

Each round:
1. **Draw Challenge:** The active team draws a challenge card.
2. **Start Timer:** Flip the sand timer.
3. **Perform Challenge:** The team attempts the dance-related challenge before time runs out.
4. **Score:** Successful completion earns points.
5. **Alternate:** Teams take turns.

## Actions

### Dance Challenges
- **Name Dances:** When the timer starts, team members must name as many dances as possible before time runs out.
- **Perform Moves:** Demonstrate specific dance moves for teammates to identify.
- **Teach Steps:** Teach a dance step to a teammate who must replicate it.
- **Dance Trivia:** Answer questions about dance history, styles, or famous dancers.
- **Physical Challenges:** Perform dance-related physical activities within the time limit.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Teams earn points for successfully completing challenges. The first team to reach the target score (or the team with the most points after all challenges are completed) wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Team Game:** The game is designed for team-based play; solo play is not supported.
- **Physical Activity:** Players should be prepared for active, physical participation.
- **Dance Knowledge:** Some challenges require knowledge of dance styles and terminology.
- **Flexible Player Count:** Works with larger groups when divided into teams.

## Player Reference

**Turn:** Draw challenge → Start timer → Perform challenge → Score

**Challenge Types:** Name dances, perform moves, teach steps, dance trivia, physical activities

**Win:** First team to reach the target score
