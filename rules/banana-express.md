## Overview

Banana Express is a children's dexterity and racing game for 2-4 players. Players race to be the first to collect bananas while overcoming three obstacles: the snake landslide, the palm tree carousel, and the adventure bridge. The game features a three-dimensional board with moving mechanical elements that create excitement and suspense.

## Components

- 3D game board with mechanical obstacles
- Player caterpillar figures
- Banana tokens
- Dice or spinner
- Obstacle mechanisms (snake landslide, palm tree carousel, adventure bridge)
- Rules booklet

## Setup

1. Assemble the 3D game board with all mechanical obstacles in place.
2. Each player chooses a caterpillar figure.
3. Place all caterpillars at the starting position.
4. Ensure all obstacle mechanisms are functioning properly.

## Turn Structure

On each turn:
1. Roll the die or spin the spinner.
2. Move your caterpillar forward the indicated number of spaces.
3. If you land on or pass through an obstacle, resolve it.
4. Collect bananas when landing on banana spaces.

## Actions

- **Move:** Advance your caterpillar along the track.
- **Navigate obstacles:** Successfully pass through the snake landslide, palm tree carousel, or adventure bridge.
- **Collect bananas:** Pick up banana tokens from designated spaces.
- **Take segments:** Players may take segments from another player's caterpillar rather than from a general pile.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to collect the required number of bananas or reach the finish wins. As caterpillars grow by collecting segments, one will eventually reach the top of the board. The banana slides down, other players' caterpillars crash, and the winner is determined.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Mechanical obstacles:** The physical obstacles create unpredictable results, adding a dexterity element.
- **Growing caterpillars:** Each caterpillar grows and shrinks as segments are gained or lost, making it unpredictable who will win.
- **Children's game:** Designed for young players with simple rules and exciting physical interactions.

## Player Reference

**Age range:** Young children

**Obstacles:** Snake landslide, Palm tree carousel, Adventure bridge

**Win condition:** First to collect required bananas or reach the top
