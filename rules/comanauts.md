## Overview

Comanauts is a cooperative adventure game set in the mind of Dr. Martin Strobal, a brilliant scientist in a coma. Players are "comanauts" who enter his subconscious, navigating through bizarre dream spaces to find clues and defeat the inner demons (I.D.s) troubling him. The game uses a unique storybook that serves as rules reference, story guide, and game board all in one. It can be played as individual sessions or a campaign.

## Components

- 1 storybook (rules, story, and game board combined)
- Character cards and standees
- Comannaut miniatures
- Dice
- Item cards, clue tokens
- Dream space tiles
- I.D. enemy cards
- Status tokens
- Event cards

## Setup

1. Choose a session or continue a campaign.
2. Each player selects a comanaut character.
3. Open the storybook to the designated starting page.
4. Place characters in the starting dream space.
5. Distribute starting items and cards.

## Turn Structure

1. **Event Phase:** Draw and resolve an event card.
2. **Action Phase:** Each player takes actions (move, explore, interact, fight).
3. **Enemy Phase:** I.D.s and their minions act.
4. **Status Phase:** Check for ongoing effects and advance any timers.

## Actions

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Move** | Travel between connected dream spaces |
| **Explore** | Search the current area for clues |
| **Interact** | Talk to dream characters, use objects |
| **Fight** | Engage I.D. minions or the I.D. itself |
| **Use Item** | Play an item card for its effect |
| **Rest** | Recover health or remove negative conditions |

### Combat
- Roll dice based on character stats and equipped items.
- Compare results to enemy defense.
- Enemies counterattack; players must manage health carefully.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- **Win a session:** Collect enough clues to identify the I.D.'s weakness, then confront and defeat it.
- **Campaign win:** Defeat all I.D.s across multiple sessions to wake Dr. Strobal.
- **Lose:** All comanauts are knocked out, or time runs out for the session.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- The storybook changes based on player choices, creating a narrative experience.
- Dream spaces are thematic and surreal, with unique rules per space.
- Each comanaut has unique abilities affecting exploration and combat.
- Items found in one session may carry over to the next in campaign mode.
- I.D.s have unique behaviors and weaknesses that must be discovered through clues.
- If a comanaut is knocked out, they can be revived by teammates at a cost.
