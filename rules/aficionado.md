## Overview

Aficionado is a fast-paced party card game where the active player reads three sentence fragments aloud, and the other players race to combine the endings of those sentences into a new, incongruous sentence. The fastest player to give the correct recombined answer wins the card. The game rewards quick thinking and creative wordplay.

## Components

- Deck of sentence fragment cards (each card containing multiple sentence parts)
- Score tracking method (collected cards serve as points)

## Setup

1. Shuffle the deck of cards.
2. Place the deck face-down in the center of the table.
3. Designate the first reader.
4. All other players are guessers.

## Turn Structure

Each round:

1. **Read:** The active player draws a card and reads three sentence fragments aloud.
2. **Race:** All other players listen and try to mentally recombine the sentence endings into a new incongruous sentence.
3. **Answer:** Players shout out their recombined sentence as quickly as possible.
4. **Award:** The first player to give the correct recombined answer wins the card.
5. **Next round:** The reader role passes to the next player.

## Actions

### Reading

- The reader draws a card and reads three sentence fragments in order.
- Each fragment contains a beginning and an ending.
- The reader must read clearly and at a consistent pace.

### Recombining

- Listening players must take the endings of the three sentences and combine them into a new sentence.
- Speed is essential — the first correct answer wins.
- The recombined sentence is typically humorous or absurd.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- Each correctly answered card is kept by the winning player as a point.
- The player with the most cards at the end of the game wins.
- The game ends when the deck is exhausted or after a predetermined number of rounds.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Speed-based:** The game rewards quick cognitive processing; there is no turn-taking among guessers.
- **Simultaneous play:** All guessers compete at the same time.
- **Correct answer required:** The recombined sentence must be correct — not just fast, but accurate.
- **Reader rotation:** The reader role rotates to ensure all players get equal opportunity to guess.
