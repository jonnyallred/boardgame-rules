## Overview

Arkham Express is a cooperative print-and-play dice game for 1-4 players set in the Lovecraftian Arkham Horror universe. Players work together to gather clues, kill monsters, close gates, and ultimately defeat Cthulhu. Each player controls a character with a unique special ability. The game distills the Arkham Horror experience into a quick, dice-driven format.

## Components

- Game board (print-and-play: map of Arkham with locations)
- 5 custom dice (with symbols: Movement, Clue, Combat, Heal, Monster, and Wild)
- Character sheets (each with a unique special ability)
- Gate tokens
- Monster tokens
- Clue tokens
- Player pawns

## Setup

1. Place the game board in the center.
2. Each player selects a character and takes the matching pawn and character sheet.
3. Place starting gates and monsters on the board as indicated.
4. Place player pawns at their starting locations.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:

1. **Roll all 5 dice.**
2. **Lock Monster dice:** Any dice showing the Monster symbol are locked and cannot be re-rolled.
3. **Re-roll:** You may re-roll any unlocked dice up to 3 additional times (4 total rolls).
4. **Resolve dice:** Use the symbols rolled to perform actions.
5. **Check for gate opening:** If 3 or more Monster symbols are showing after any roll, a new gate opens and a monster appears.

## Actions

**Movement (Movement symbols):** Move your pawn to an adjacent location on the board. Each movement symbol moves you one space.

**Gather Clues (Clue symbols):** Collect clue tokens at your current location. Clues are used to close gates.

**Combat (Combat symbols):** Fight monsters at your current location. Each combat symbol deals damage to a monster. Monsters require a certain number of combat symbols to defeat.

**Heal (Heal symbols):** Restore health/sanity to yourself or another player at the same location.

**Close Gates:** Spend collected clue tokens to close an open gate at your location. Closing a gate earns the team progress toward victory.

**Monster Symbol:** Locked when rolled. If 3 Monster symbols appear, a gate opens with a monster.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

**Win:** Players win by closing 6 gates and then defeating the Great Old One (Cthulhu) in a final battle.

**Lose:** Players lose if:
- 6 gates are open simultaneously on the board.
- Any player is eliminated (reduced to 0 health/sanity with no way to heal).
- The Great Old One awakens before players are ready.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Character Abilities:** Each character has a unique special ability that modifies dice or provides bonus actions (e.g., extra re-rolls, bonus movement, combat bonuses).
- **Monster Dice Lock:** Monster symbols cannot be re-rolled once they appear. This creates escalating tension as more dice get locked.
- **Cooperative Play:** Players should coordinate movement and clue-gathering to efficiently close gates.
- **Gate Surge:** If a new gate would open on a location that already has a gate, a monster surge occurs, placing additional monsters.
- **Solo Mode:** One player controls a single character. The game is fully soloable.
- **Print-and-Play:** This is a fan-designed print-and-play game, not a commercial release.
