## Overview

Arydia: The Paths We Dare Tread is a cooperative open-world adventure game for 1-4 players by Far Off Games. Players explore a dynamically generated world, encountering creatures, completing quests, and developing their characters along one of several paths (Valor, Arcana, Light, Inspiration, Wandering, Shadow). The game features a branching narrative, tactical combat, and sandbox-style exploration with a story that evolves based on player decisions.

## Components

- Character boards and miniatures
- Map tiles (for dynamically building the world)
- Encounter cards and quest cards
- Combat cards and dice
- Path-specific ability decks (Valor, Arcana, Light, Inspiration, Wandering, Shadow)
- Enemy miniatures and stat cards
- Resource tokens
- Narrative booklet
- Rules booklet and Quick Start Guide

## Setup

1. Select characters and their starting paths.
2. Place starting map tiles to form the initial world area.
3. Shuffle and prepare encounter, quest, and combat decks.
4. Set up character boards with starting equipment and abilities.
5. Place initial quest markers and narrative elements.

## Turn Structure

Each round, players take turns performing actions with their characters:

1. **Movement:** Move your character across map tiles.
2. **Explore:** Reveal new map tiles when entering unexplored areas.
3. **Encounter:** Resolve encounters triggered by exploration or quest events.
4. **Combat:** When engaging enemies, use tactical card-based combat.
5. **Rest/Recover:** Heal and prepare for future challenges.

## Actions

- **Move:** Travel across the map to reach new locations.
- **Explore:** Reveal new terrain tiles, expanding the game world.
- **Quest actions:** Complete objectives from active quest cards.
- **Combat:** Engage enemies using path-specific abilities and combat cards.
- **Interact:** Trade, craft, or interact with narrative elements and NPCs.
- **Develop character:** Gain experience and unlock new abilities along your chosen path.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The game is cooperative; all players win or lose together. Victory is achieved by completing the main quest line and resolving the narrative's central conflict. Failure occurs if all players are defeated or certain story conditions trigger a loss.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Open world:** The map is built dynamically through exploration, so no two games look the same.
- **Character paths:** Each path (Valor, Arcana, Light, etc.) provides unique abilities and combat styles.
- **Narrative branching:** Player decisions affect the story, leading to different encounters and outcomes.
- **Solo play:** Fully supported as a single-player experience.
- **Campaign mode:** The game can be played as a series of connected sessions with persistent character progress.

## Player Reference

**Player count:** 1-4 (cooperative)

**Turn actions:** Move, Explore, Encounter, Combat, Rest, Quest

**Win condition:** Complete the main quest line cooperatively
