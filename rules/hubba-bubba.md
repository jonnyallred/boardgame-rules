## Overview

Hubba + Bubba is a children's roll-and-move adventure board game where players embark on a voyage through Hubba Bubba land. The main road passes through 100 locations, but shortcuts are available for players willing to take risks. Players roll dice and navigate the board, choosing between the safe main road and dangerous but faster shortcuts. The first player to reach the end of the journey wins.

## Components

- 1 Game Board (with 100 locations on the main road plus shortcut paths)
- Player tokens (1 per player)
- Die/dice
- Event/hazard cards or spaces

## Setup

1. Place the game board centrally.
2. Each player selects a token and places it at the start.
3. Determine who goes first.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Roll the die** and move your token forward by the number shown.
2. **Resolve the space** you land on (some spaces have special effects).
3. **Shortcut decision** — If you land on or near a shortcut entrance, you may choose to take it.

## Actions

### Moving Along the Main Road
- Move forward the number of spaces shown on the die.
- The main road is safe but takes all 100 locations to complete.

### Taking Shortcuts
- Shortcuts offer a faster route but come with risks.
- Landing on a shortcut entrance allows you to choose the faster path.
- Hazards on shortcuts may send you backward, cost a turn, or have other penalties.

### Special Spaces
- Certain locations on the board have events or effects when landed on.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- The first player to reach the end of the board (complete the voyage through Hubba Bubba land) wins.
- No scoring — it is a pure race game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Risk vs. safety:** Shortcuts are faster but dangerous; the main road is slower but predictable.
- **100 locations:** The full main road path covers all 100 locations.
- **Children's game:** Simple mechanics suitable for younger players.
- **Branded theme:** The Hubba Bubba candy brand provides the game's theming.
