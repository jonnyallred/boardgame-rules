## Overview

Don't Drop the Soap is a dark-humored, fast-paced card game of hot potato set in a prison shower. There are no winners, just one loser -- the player who is forced to play a soap card. Players try to empty their hands or force others to pick up cards.

## Components

- 59 cards (single deck including soap cards, action cards, and other card types)

## Setup

1. Someone takes the role of dealer.
2. Give one soap card to the player with "the best butt."
3. Shuffle the deck and deal 5 cards to each player (4 cards if playing with 6 players).
4. Place the deck face-down in the middle of the table.
5. The player with the extra soap card has the first turn.

## Turn Structure

On each turn:
1. Play exactly one card from your hand into the discard pile.
2. Resolve the card's effect.
3. You never draw a card from the deck unless forced to by a card effect.

## Actions

Each card has a specific effect that must be resolved when played. Soap cards can only be played if you have no other options or cards to play.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- If you get rid of all your cards, you are "safe" and can no longer be targeted by other players.
- The game ends when a player loses by either:
  - Playing a soap card (no other options)
  - Being the last non-safe player in the game
- The loser starts the next round with the extra soap card.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- The Lawyer card is the only card that can target a "safe" player.
- If the deck runs out of cards, do NOT reshuffle the discard pile. Finish the game with the cards in hand.
- If a card's conditions cannot be met, play the card but ignore its effect.
  - Example: Play a "Snitch" card even if you have no other cards in hand.
  - Example: Play a "Prison bitch" card even if there are no more cards in the deck.
  - Example: Play "Smokes" even if you have no other cards to trade.
