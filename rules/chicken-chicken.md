## Overview

Chicken Chicken is a memory and reaction game where players must silently track the number of Egg cards currently "in play" as cards are revealed from a deck one at a time. When a player believes there are 5 or more Eggs in play, they slap the pile to score a point. The first player to collect 3 points and assemble a complete egg token wins.

## Components

- 52 cards total:
  - 20 Chicken Egg cards (15 in base game, 5 more with Farmer variant)
  - 10 Hen cards
  - 10 Fox cards
  - 2 Dog cards (variant)
  - 2 Disguised Fox cards (variant)
  - 2 Duck cards (variant)
  - 2 Worm cards (variant)
  - 2 Ostrich Egg cards (variant)
  - 1 Rooster card (variant)
  - 1 Farmer card (variant)
- 8 Egg tokens (each in 3-piece segments, one set per player color)

## Setup

1. Construct the base deck using: 15 Chicken Eggs, 10 Hens, and 10 Foxes. Return remaining cards to the box.
2. Shuffle the deck and place it within reach of all players.
3. Give each player 3 egg segments of the same color to track their score.

## Turn Structure

The game has no individual turns in the traditional sense. One player serves as the **Head Farmer** and controls the card reveal.

### Card Reveal Phase

The Head Farmer slowly reveals cards from the deck, one by one, stacking them face-up so only the most recent card is visible. All players silently count how many Egg cards are currently "in play."

### Card Interactions

- **Egg card revealed:** The Egg is now "in play" -- add 1 to mental count.
- **Hen card revealed (after an Egg):** The Hen "sits on" an Egg, removing it from play -- subtract 1 from count. A Hen can only sit on 1 Egg.
- **Fox card revealed (after a Hen on an Egg):** The Fox scares away a Hen, putting the Egg back "in play" -- add 1 to count. A Fox can only scare away 1 Hen.
- **Hen with no Egg available:** No effect; discard.
- **Fox with no Hen sitting on an Egg:** No effect; discard.

## Actions

### Slap the Pile

When any player believes there are 5 or more Eggs in play, they slap the pile of face-up cards. The first player to slap scores 1 point and collects one of their colored egg segments.

### Issue a Challenge

If another player believes the slapper was wrong (fewer than 5 Eggs in play), they may declare a challenge. The Head Farmer replays the cards in original order to demonstrate the count.

- **Slapper was correct:** Slapper scores 1 point as usual. Each challenger loses 1 point.
- **Slapper was incorrect:** Slapper neither gains nor loses points. Each challenger scores 1 point and collects an egg segment.
- Players can never go below 0 points.

### End of Round

After a slap (resolved with any challenges), gather all cards, shuffle the deck, and begin a new round. The player to the left of the current Head Farmer becomes the new Head Farmer.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Once any player has collected 3 points and assembled a complete egg token, they win the game. If multiple players complete their eggs in the same round, they share the victory.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

### Bonus Card Variants (add after learning the base game)

| Card | Quantity | Effect |
|------|----------|--------|
| Roger the Farmer | x1 | "Collects" all Eggs currently in play (subtract all). Does NOT collect Eggs that Hens are sitting on. Add 5 extra Egg cards to the deck. |
| Aspen the Dog | x2 | Chases away the next Fox (negating its effect). Stays until a Fox appears. |
| Vanessa the Disguised Fox | x2 | Replaces a regular Fox. Same effect as Fox but NOT stopped by the Dog. |
| Dolly the Duck | x2 | All players must say "Quack!" (or "Quack! Quack!" for second appearance). No effect on egg count. |
| Rico the Rooster | x1 | Immediately ends the round. First player to slap must announce the current egg count. Challenges proceed as normal. |
| Tiger the Worm | x2 | Distracts the next Hen from sitting on an Egg (negates the Hen's effect). |
| Double the Ostrich Egg | x2 | Counts as 2 Eggs. A Hen cannot sit on it. The Farmer can collect it. |

**Warning:** Do not add too many variant cards at once.

## Player Reference

**Base card interactions:**

| Card | Effect on Egg Count |
|------|-------------------|
| Egg | +1 (in play) |
| Hen (with Egg available) | -1 (sits on Egg) |
| Fox (with sitting Hen) | +1 (scares Hen, Egg returns) |
| Hen (no Egg) | No effect |
| Fox (no sitting Hen) | No effect |

**Slap at 5+ Eggs in play.**

| Outcome | Slapper | Challengers |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Correct slap | +1 point | -1 point each |
| Incorrect slap | 0 points | +1 point each |

**Win: 3 points = complete egg token.**
