## Overview

Aku Ankka is a Finnish trivia board game produced in cooperation with the Aku Ankka (Donald Duck) comics magazine. Players test their knowledge of Duckburg and its inhabitants while navigating a game board, visiting locations, and ultimately racing to capture the Number One Dime and return it to the Money Bin.

## Components

- Game board (depicting Duckburg locations)
- Player tokens
- Trivia question cards (covering Duckburg knowledge)
- Number One Dime token
- Location markers
- Action point tracker

## Setup

1. Place the game board in the center of the table.
2. Each player selects a token and places it at the starting position.
3. Shuffle trivia question cards and place them in a draw pile.
4. Place the Number One Dime on its designated space.
5. Determine first player.

## Turn Structure

On each turn, a player receives **5 action points** which can be spent on:

1. **Moving** — Spend action points to move across the board.
2. **Answering trivia questions** — Answer questions about Duckburg to earn rewards or progress.
3. **Using special actions** — Spend action points on other available options as the board allows.

## Actions

### Movement

- Each action point spent allows movement to an adjacent space.
- Players must navigate the board to visit required locations.

### Trivia Questions

- Players answer trivia questions related to the Donald Duck / Duckburg universe.
- Correct answers may earn extra action points, rewards, or progress toward objectives.

### Visiting Locations

- Each player must visit all of their designated locations on the board.
- Visiting a location may require answering a trivia question or spending action points.

### Capturing the Number One Dime

- After visiting all required locations, a player may attempt to capture the Number One Dime from another player or its starting location.
- Once captured, the player must return to the Money Bin with the dime.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The winner is the first player to:
1. Visit all their designated locations on the board,
2. Capture the Number One Dime,
3. Return the Number One Dime to Scrooge McDuck's Money Bin.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Finnish language:** The game is produced in Finnish for the Finnish market, tied to the popular Aku Ankka comics magazine.
- **5 action points per turn:** Players must strategically allocate their limited action points between movement, answering questions, and other actions.
- **Dime theft:** The Number One Dime can potentially be taken from another player, creating a chase element.
