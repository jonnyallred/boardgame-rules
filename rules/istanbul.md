# Istanbul

## Overview

Players lead a merchant and four assistants through a 4x4 grid of bazaar locations. To perform an action at a location, your merchant must leave behind or pick up an assistant. The goal is to be the first to collect 5 rubies (6 in a 2-player game). Rubies are obtained from various locations by paying goods, coins, or completing other requirements.

## Components

- 16 Place tiles (form a 4x4 grid)
- 5 Wheelbarrows (player boards) with goods indicators
- 5 sets of Wheelbarrow extensions (3 per player)
- Rubies
- 2 Dice
- Mail indicators
- Coins (Lira): 1 (30x), 5 (15x), 10 (12x)
- Bonus cards (26)
- Mosque tiles (16)
- Demand tiles (10: 5 dark, 5 light)
- Governor (purple) and Smuggler (black) tokens
- Starting player marker
- Per player: 1 Merchant disc, 5 Assistants, 1 Family member, 4 Goods indicators

## Setup

1. Lay out 16 Place tiles in a 4x4 grid (use "Short paths" layout for first game).
2. Randomly determine start player (gets 2 Lira); each subsequent player clockwise gets 1 more Lira.
3. Each player draws 1 Bonus card.
4. Stack your 4 Assistants with Merchant on top; place on Fountain (Place 7).
5. Place Family members on Police Station (Place 12).
6. Place rubies and extensions on appropriate places.
7. Roll dice to place Governor and Smuggler on random tiles.
8. Set up Mosque tiles, Market demand tiles, Mail indicators, etc.

**2-player special:** Place neutral Merchant discs on Mosques 14/15 and Gemstone Dealer 16. Need 6 rubies to win.

## Turn Structure

On your turn, execute these phases in order:

### 1. Movement
Move your Merchant stack 1 or 2 Places (orthogonally, not diagonally). Must end on a different Place.
- **If your Assistant is at the target:** Pick them up (add to bottom of stack).
- **If no Assistant is there:** Leave one from the bottom of your stack. If you have none to leave, your turn ends immediately (exception: Fountain).

### 2. Encounters with Other Merchants
Pay 2 Lira to each other Merchant at the target Place. If you cannot or will not pay, your turn ends. Exception: no payment required at the Fountain.

### 3. Action
Carry out the action of the target Place.

### 4. Encounters with Family Members, Governor, Smuggler
- **Other Family members:** Catch them, send to Police Station. Receive 1 Bonus card or 3 Lira per catch.
- **Governor:** May draw 1 Bonus card (pay 2 Lira or discard 1 card). Roll dice to relocate Governor.
- **Smuggler:** May gain 1 good of choice (pay 2 Lira or 1 good). Roll dice to relocate Smuggler.

## Actions

### Place Actions

| # | Place | Action |
|---|-------|--------|
| 1 | Wainwright | Pay 7 Lira for a Wheelbarrow extension. 3rd extension = 1 Ruby. |
| 2-4 | Warehouses (Fabric/Spice/Fruit) | Fill the corresponding good to maximum. |
| 5 | Post Office | Receive resources shown on 4 uncovered spaces. Move leftmost Mail indicator down. |
| 6 | Caravansary | Take 2 Bonus cards, discard 1. May take from discard pile. |
| 7 | Fountain | Return any number of Assistants to your stack. No assistant needed. No merchant payment. |
| 8 | Black Market | Gain 1 red/yellow/green good. Roll dice: 7-8 = +1 blue, 9-10 = +2 blue, 11-12 = +3 blue. |
| 9 | Tea House | Announce a number 3-12, roll dice. Meet/exceed = take that many Lira. Fail = take 2 Lira. |
| 10/11 | Large/Small Market | Sell 1-5 depicted goods for Lira per the table. Rotate demand tile. |
| 12 | Police Station | Send Family member to any Place; carry out that Place's action (no encounters). |
| 13 | Sultan's Palace | Deliver required goods to earn next Ruby. Cost increases each time. |
| 14/15 | Small/Great Mosque | Acquire Mosque tile (need depicted goods, pay 1). Both tiles from one Mosque = 1 Ruby. |
| 16 | Gemstone Dealer | Pay Lira equal to largest uncovered number on track. Take next Ruby. |

### Mosque Tile Abilities

| Color | Ability |
|-------|--------|
| Red | At Tea House/Black Market: turn 1 die to "4" or re-roll both dice. |
| Blue | Immediately gain 5th Assistant. |
| Green | At Warehouses: pay 2 Lira for 1 extra good of any type. |
| Yellow | Once per turn: pay 2 Lira to return 1 Assistant to your stack. |

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- First player to collect **5 Rubies** (6 in 2-player) triggers game end.
- Complete the current round, then each player may use leftover Bonus cards for goods/money.
- **Most Rubies wins.** Tiebreakers: most Lira, then most goods, then most Bonus cards.

### Ruby Sources
1. Wainwright: complete 3rd Wheelbarrow extension
2. Sultan's Palace: deliver goods
3. Each Mosque: collect both tiles from one Mosque
4. Gemstone Dealer: pay increasing Lira
5. Some Bonus cards

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- You cannot stay at the same Place (must move 1 or 2).
- If you cannot leave an Assistant and are not at the Fountain, your turn ends after movement.
- At the Fountain, you do not need an Assistant and do not pay other Merchants.
- Bonus cards may be played at any time during your turn (before, during, or after actions, with restrictions noted on cards).
- In 2-player games, neutral Merchants cost 2 Lira to the supply and are then relocated by dice roll.
- 2 players: need 6 rubies. 3-5 players: need 5 rubies.
