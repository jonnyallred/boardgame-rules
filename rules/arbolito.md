## Overview

Arbolito is a card-stacking dexterity game designed by Arno Steinwender and published by Red Cat Games in 2024. Players compete to create a colorful tree by hanging leaf cards on a 3D tree structure. Each leaf card has one or more color blocks that must be carefully hung on the tree branches without unbalancing it. Players race to get rid of their cards by making combos and using special animal effects, but the leaves can fall off at any moment, penalizing clumsy players. The game also includes a solo mode.

## Components

- 54 leaf cards (with 1, 2, or 3 color blocks each)
- 12 acorn tokens
- 2 assembly trees (3D tree structures)
- 6 effect tokens
- Rulebook

## Setup

1. Assemble one or both tree structures and place in the center of the table.
2. Shuffle the 54 leaf cards.
3. Deal an equal number of leaf cards to each player.
4. Place acorn tokens and effect tokens within reach.
5. Determine the first player.

## Turn Structure

On each turn, a player must:

1. **Hang a leaf card** on the tree, matching color requirements on the branches.
2. **Apply any animal effects** shown on the card played.
3. **Resolve fallen leaves:** If any leaves fall from the tree, the active player must pick them up and add them to their hand.

## Actions

### Hanging Leaves

- Play one leaf card from your hand by physically hanging it on a branch of the tree.
- Cards have 1, 2, or 3 color blocks that must align with the branch color rules.
- The card must balance on the tree — if it falls (or causes other cards to fall), you pick up all fallen cards.

### Color Combos

- Playing cards that match or chain colors can create combos.
- Combos allow you to play additional cards on the same turn, helping you empty your hand faster.

### Animal Effects

- Some leaf cards have animal icons that trigger special effects when played.
- Effects can disrupt opponents (forcing them to draw extra cards or skip turns) or benefit you (extra plays, protection from penalties).
- The 6 effect tokens track or modify these animal powers.

### Acorn Tokens

- Acorn tokens provide special bonuses or protections during the game.
- Managing your acorn tokens adds a strategic layer on top of the dexterity challenge.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to get rid of all their leaf cards wins the game. If leaves fall from the tree on your turn, you must add them to your hand, pushing you further from victory.

**Solo mode:** Try to hang the entire deck of 54 cards on the tree without any falling off.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Fallen leaf penalty:** Any cards that fall from the tree on your turn are added to your hand — even if another player's card was poorly placed and your card merely tipped it.
- **Dexterity requirement:** Physical skill is central — steady hands and careful placement are essential.
- **Animal effects vary:** Different animals have different powers; learning what each does is important for strategy.
- **Solo challenge:** The solo mode tasks you with hanging all 54 cards on the tree, testing dexterity without competitive pressure.
- **Two trees available:** The two assembly trees may allow for larger games or alternate configurations.
- **Ages 7+:** Simple enough for children but entertaining for all ages.
