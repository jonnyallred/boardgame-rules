## Overview

Potion Explosion is a marble-matching engine-building game set in a sorcery academy. Players take ingredient marbles from a dispenser, triggering chain-reaction explosions when same-colored marbles collide. Collected ingredients fill potion recipes; completed potions grant magical powers and score points. The player with the most points after enough Skill Tokens are awarded wins.

## Components

- 1 plastic dispenser with marble tracks
- 80 ingredient marbles (20 each of 4 colors: Unicorn Tears, Ogre Mucus, Dragon Smoke, Fairy Dandruff)
- 64 potion tiles (8 kinds with various powers)
- 4 desk boards
- 1 first player token
- 15 Skill tokens (worth 4 points each)
- 21 Little Help tokens

## Setup

1. Assemble the dispenser and fill tracks randomly with marbles.
2. Separate starter potion tiles (marked with special symbol) from regular potions. Remove 2 potion types randomly; use the remaining 6 types.
3. Deal 2 starter potions per player via draft (clockwise then counterclockwise).
4. Each player places 2 starter potions on their desk board (up to 2 potions being brewed at a time).
5. Place remaining potions in face-up stacks by type.
6. Place Skill tokens (based on player count) and Little Help tokens nearby.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Pick an ingredient marble** from the dispenser.
2. **Resolve explosions** (chain reactions from same-colored collisions).
3. **Place ingredients** on your potion tiles or in your pool (up to 3 stored in pool).
4. **Use completed potions** (optional, any time during your turn).
5. **Discard excess ingredients** (pool maximum is 3).
6. **Take new potion tiles** if you have empty brewing slots.

## Actions

### Taking a Marble
Choose any marble from the dispenser. Remaining marbles slide down to fill gaps.

### Explosions
When same-colored marbles collide after a marble is removed, an explosion occurs: take all connected same-colored marbles from that collision group. Chain reactions can trigger further explosions. All explosion marbles are collected for free.

### Little Help
You may ask for a "Little Help" once per turn: take any 1 marble from the dispenser without triggering explosions. Take a Little Help token (worth -2 points at end of game).

### Completing Potions
When all holes on a potion tile are filled with correct-colored marbles, the potion is complete. Return the marbles to the dispenser, flip the tile, and gain its power. Take a new potion tile for your empty slot.

### Potion Powers
Each of the 8 potion types grants a unique power when consumed (used). Completed potions can be drunk on any of your future turns for their effect. Once drunk, the potion is flipped but still scores points.

### Skill Tokens
When you complete 3 potions of the same type OR 5 potions of 5 different types, gain 1 Skill Token (worth 4 points).

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

When the required number of Skill Tokens have been awarded (based on player count), the end of game is triggered. Finish the current round.

**Final Scoring:**
- Points from completed potion tiles (printed on each tile)
- +4 points per Skill Token
- -2 points per Little Help token

The player with the most points wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Maximum 3 ingredients stored in your pool between turns.
- Maximum 2 potions being brewed at any time on your desk.
- Potions can be used (drunk) on the same turn they are completed.
- Little Help: 1 per turn, costs -2 points.
- When refilling the dispenser, add marbles randomly.

## Player Reference

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Pick marble | Take 1 from dispenser; resolve all explosions |
| Little Help | Take 1 extra marble (no explosions), -2 VP penalty |
| Place ingredients | Fill potion recipes or store in pool (max 3) |
| Complete potion | All holes filled; return marbles, gain power |
| Drink potion | Use completed potion's power |
| Skill Token | 3 same type OR 5 different types = +4 VP |
