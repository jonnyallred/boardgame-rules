## Overview

Havana (La Habana) is a card-driven resource management game set in Cuba's capital. Players use action cards to collect construction materials, rubble, pesos, and workers to construct buildings and earn prestige points. The two-digit number formed by your played cards determines turn order. The first player to reach the minimum prestige points wins.

## Components

- 80 wooden bricks (4 sets of 10 colored building materials + 40 grey rubble)
- 4 sets of 13 action cards (1 per player)
- 36 structure cards
- 1 supply reminder card
- 15 worker tokens
- 60 peso coins
- 1 burlap bag

## Setup

1. Each player takes 13 action cards (identical backs) + 1 peso + 1 grey rubble brick.
2. Mix remaining bricks in the bag.
3. Place pesos and workers in separate accessible piles.
4. Shuffle structure cards, make 2 rows of 6 face up. Ensure at least one 1, 2, or 3 PP structure is at a row end.
5. Place supply reminder card centrally with 3 pesos + 3 random bricks from the bag on it.

## Turn Structure

### First Round
Each player chooses 2 action cards from hand, places them face down. All reveal simultaneously.

### Subsequent Rounds (4 Phases)
**Phase 1 -- Initiative:** Form a two-digit number from your two cards (lower number on left). Lowest initiative goes first.

**Phase 2 -- Construction and Actions:** In initiative order, perform your 2 actions (in any order). Then build as many structures as you can afford. Only structures at the ends of rows may be built.

**Phase 3 -- Supply:** Add 3 pesos + 3 random bricks from the bag to the center of the table.

**Phase 4 -- Replacement:** Keep 1 of your 2 played action cards (it carries over). Choose 1 new card from hand to replace the discarded one. Reveal new cards simultaneously. When only 2 cards remain in hand, reclaim your discard pile.

## Actions

| Card | Action |
|------|--------|
| 0 | No effect (lowers initiative) |
| 1 | Protection from theft this round |
| 2 | Take 1 peso from reserve |
| 2 | Swap 1 hand card for 1 discard pile card (show to others) |
| 3 | Remove 1 end-of-row structure (once per round total) |
| 3 | Take all rubble from center of table |
| 4 | Take 2 workers (1 if not first to play this action) |
| 4 | Take 1 worker; required for structures with architect symbol (not consumed) |
| 5 | Steal 2 pesos from next player (3 if they have 6+ pesos) |
| 6 | Steal 1 brick from next player (2 if they have 4+ materials) |
| 7 | Draw 2 random bricks or choose 1 from bag (1 random if not first) |
| 8 | Take half the pesos from center (round up) |
| 9 | Take half the materials and half the rubble from center (round up both) |

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The game ends immediately when a player reaches:
- **25 PP** for 2 players
- **20 PP** for 3 players
- **15 PP** for 4 players

If all structures are built or all bricks used, the player with the most PP wins.

### Building Structures
- Pay the construction cost shown on the structure card.
- Only structures at either end of a row can be built.
- Building materials and rubble are discarded to the box; pesos and workers return to the reserve.
- When a row drops to 2 structures, draw 4 new structures and place them between the remaining two.

### 5-for-1 Rule
During construction: 5 pesos = 1 worker (not reverse); 5 rubble = 1 building material (not reverse). May be used multiple times.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Initiative ties:** Broken by fewest PP, then fewest building materials, then fewest pesos, then fewest workers, then fewest rubble, then youngest player.
- **Theft targets:** Cards 5 and 6 steal from the player going after you in initiative order.
- **Card 1 protection:** Blocks all theft attempts against you for the round.
- **Card 4 (architect):** Acts as a permanent qualifier for building, not consumed as a resource.
- **Card 3 (demolish):** Only 1 structure can be removed per round across all players.
- **Cards 4 and 7:** First player to use the action gets the better reward.

## Player Reference

**Initiative:** Two-digit number from your cards (lower digit left). Lowest goes first.

**Building:** Pay costs on structure card. Materials/rubble consumed; pesos/workers returned.

**5-for-1 Conversions:** 5 pesos -> 1 worker | 5 rubble -> 1 material (one direction only)

**Victory:** First to 25/20/15 PP (2/3/4 players)
