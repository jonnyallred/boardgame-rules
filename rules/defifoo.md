## Overview

Defifoo is a French party game for 3-18 players where teams compete across 150 wacky challenges. Two teams face off while a third team or individual acts as referee. Challenges include drawing, miming, singing, guessing, dancing, and other physical or creative activities. The first team to win 7 challenges wins the game. The game includes a variety of physical props (dice, spinning tops, tape measures, etc.) to support its diverse challenge types.

## Components

- 150 challenge cards (with 240 challenges total)
- 10 dice
- 10 chips/tokens
- 2 spinning tops (toupies)
- 2 pencils
- 2 notepads
- 2 tape measures
- 1 30-second hourglass (sand timer)
- Rules booklet

## Setup

1. Divide players into two competing teams (roughly equal size) and optionally a referee team.
2. Place all challenge cards in a draw pile.
3. Set out all props (dice, chips, spinning tops, pencils, notepads, tape measures, hourglass) within reach.
4. Determine which team goes first.

## Turn Structure

Each round:
1. **Draw a Challenge:** The active team draws a challenge card.
2. **Read the Challenge:** The referee (or a member of the opposing team) reads the challenge aloud.
3. **Perform the Challenge:** The active team attempts the challenge, often against a 30-second timer.
4. **Judge the Result:** The referee or opposing team judges whether the challenge was successfully completed.
5. **Score:** A successful challenge earns the team one point toward the 7 needed to win.
6. **Alternate:** Teams take turns drawing and attempting challenges.

## Actions

### Challenge Types
- **Drawing:** Sketch something for teammates to guess.
- **Miming:** Act out a prompt without speaking.
- **Singing:** Perform a song or musical challenge.
- **Guessing:** Identify answers from clues.
- **Dancing:** Perform a dance move or routine.
- **Physical Challenges:** Use props (dice, spinning tops, tape measures) for dexterity or accuracy tasks.
- **Creative Challenges:** Improvise, invent, or perform in unusual ways.

### Using Props
- Different challenges require different props from the component set.
- The challenge card specifies which props are needed.

### Timer
- Many challenges must be completed within the 30-second hourglass timer.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first team to successfully complete 7 challenges wins the game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Referee Role:** A third team or individual acts as impartial referee, judging challenge completion. If no referee is available, the opposing team judges.
- **Challenge Variety:** With 240 challenges across 150 cards, the game offers extensive replayability.
- **Scalability:** Works with groups from 3 to 18 players, making it suitable for parties and large gatherings.
- **French Language:** The game is primarily published in French; challenges are in French.
- **Multiple Editions:** Several color-coded editions (Classic, Marron, Vert, Violet, Bleu) expand the challenge pool.
