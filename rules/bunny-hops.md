## Overview

Bunny Hops! is a party game where teams compete by guessing, miming, and singing in response to funny questions and challenges. Teams take turns having one member perform an activity (acting out a prompt, singing, or answering a question) while teammates try to guess correctly. The team that earns the most points through successful guesses wins.

## Components

- Challenge cards (with various prompts for guessing, miming, and singing)
- Timer
- Score tracking board or tokens
- Dice or spinner for category selection

## Setup

1. Divide players into two or more teams of equal size.
2. Shuffle the challenge cards and place them in a draw pile.
3. Set up score tracking.
4. Determine which team goes first.

## Turn Structure

On each turn:
1. One player from the active team draws a challenge card.
2. The card specifies the type of challenge: **Guess**, **Mime**, or **Sing**.
3. The active player performs the challenge while their teammates attempt to guess the answer.
4. If the team guesses correctly before time runs out, they earn a point.
5. Play passes to the next team.

## Actions

**Guess Challenges:**
- The active player gives verbal clues (without saying the target word or phrase) while teammates try to guess.

**Mime Challenges:**
- The active player acts out the prompt without speaking while teammates guess.

**Sing Challenges:**
- The active player must sing or hum a song related to the prompt while teammates identify it.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Teams earn points for each correct guess within the time limit. The game continues for a predetermined number of rounds. The team with the most points at the end wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **No Speaking During Mime:** The performing player must not speak during mime challenges — only physical acting is allowed.
- **No Target Words During Guess:** When giving verbal clues, the performing player cannot say the target word or any part of it.
- **Timer:** All challenges must be completed within the time limit. If the timer runs out, the team scores no points for that round.
- **Team Size:** Works best with teams of 3-4 players each, but accommodates larger groups.
- **Party Atmosphere:** The game is designed for laughs and social interaction, with deliberately funny and challenging prompts.
