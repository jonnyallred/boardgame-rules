# My First Castle Panic

## Overview

My First Castle Panic is a cooperative game for young children where players work together to catch monsters before they reach and destroy the castle. Players match cards by color and shape to catch monsters on a linear path. If all monsters are caught, everyone wins. If a monster reaches the castle, everyone loses. No reading is required.

## Components

- 1 board (linear path with colored/shaped spaces)
- 1 castle (3 pieces: front + 2 sides)
- 1 wall (3 pieces: front + 2 sides)
- 26 Castle cards
- 16 monster tokens (including 3 starting monsters with stars)
- 4 How-to-Play reference cards
- 1 Monster Dungeon (bottom of box)

## Setup

1. Place the castle in the castle space, wall in the wall space.
2. Place the 3 star monsters on the first 3 spaces starting from START.
3. Mix remaining monsters face down next to the board.
4. Shuffle Castle cards and deal 1 face up to each player. Place rest as draw deck.
5. Keep the bottom of the box nearby as the Monster Dungeon.
6. Youngest player goes first; play clockwise.

## Turn Structure

Each turn has 4 steps in order:

1. **Draw 1 card** from the draw pile.
2. **Play 1 card** from your hand (or ask for help).
3. **Move all monsters** 1 space closer to the castle.
4. **Draw 1 monster** token and place it on the START space.

## Actions

### Playing Cards

To catch a monster, play a card matching BOTH the **color** AND **shape** of the space the monster occupies.

| Card Type | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Regular card** | Matches 1 monster on the matching color + shape space |
| **Hero card** | Matches any shape of the shown color |
| **Any-color card** | Matches any color of the shown shape |
| **Wall card** | Rebuilds the wall if knocked flat |
| **Boot card** | Send any monster back face down into the monster pile |

### Asking for Help

Instead of playing your own card, you may ask 1 other player to play a matching card from their hand. Only 1 card may be played per turn total.

### Monster Types

| Monster | Special Effect |
|---------|---------------|
| **Regular** | No special effect |
| **Shover** | After being placed on START, move ALL monsters 1 additional space toward the castle |
| **Runner** | Placed on the 2nd space instead of START |
| **Super Monster** | Requires 2 hits (2 matching cards on separate turns) to catch |

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

**Win**: All monsters are caught (dungeon'd) and no more monsters remain in the pile.

**Lose**: A monster reaches the castle and knocks it flat.

### Wall and Castle

- If a monster reaches the wall, both the wall and monster are knocked flat. The monster goes to the Dungeon and the wall is set aside.
- If a monster reaches the castle (wall already destroyed), the castle is knocked flat and all players lose.
- Monsters cannot land in an empty wall space.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- If the draw pile runs out, shuffle discards to form a new draw pile.
- No hand limit.
- Players may show cards and discuss strategy openly (cooperative).
- If no player has a matching card, skip the Play step.
- The **More Help Variation**: Allow any player to play a matching card on any turn, not just when asked.
