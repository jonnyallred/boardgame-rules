## Overview

Angry Birds: Knock on Wood is a dexterity game based on the popular mobile game. Players take turns selecting Mission Cards, building structures from blocks and pigs, and launching birds via a slingshot to knock them down. The first player to reach 1,000 points wins.

## Components

- 3 Angry Birds (projectiles)
- 14 Building blocks (2 squares, 2 triangles, 4 small girders, 6 larger girders)
- 4 Pigs
- 1 Bonus Points Star
- 1 Bonus Points Egg
- 40 Mission Cards (4 levels)
- 16 Points Cards
- 1 Bird Launcher (slingshot)

## Setup

1. Shuffle each level of Mission Cards individually and place face down in separate piles.
2. Place Points Cards near the Mission Cards.
3. Place all birds, pigs, bonus pieces, bird launcher, and building blocks within reach of all players.

## Turn Structure

1. On your turn, choose a Mission Card from any of the 4 levels.
2. Give the card to the next player, who builds the castle shown on the card.
3. Launch birds at the castle using the slingshot to knock down pigs.
4. Score points if all pigs are knocked over.
5. Play passes clockwise.

## Actions

### Choosing a Mission Card

Select from any of the 4 difficulty levels:

| Level | Difficulty | Points |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Easy | 100 |
| 2 | Medium | 200 |
| 3 | Hard | 300 |
| Freestyle | Variable | 200 or 300 |

### Building the Castle

- Levels 1-3: Build EXACTLY as shown on the card.
- Freestyle: Build freely using the pieces shown on the card, following these rules:
  - Build up and out only (no pieces in front or behind)
  - Must use ALL pieces shown on the card
  - No piece may be farther than 2 inches from another piece
  - Castle cannot be more than 1 foot long
  - Pigs and bonus pieces must be ON a building block (not on the playing surface)

Build the castle facing the shooter. Any side of a girder can face forward.

### Launching Birds

- Place the launcher at least 1 foot from the front of the castle.
- Shoot birds in the order shown on the card (left to right).
- If a bird appears twice on a card, use the same bird twice.
- Do not move any pieces until all pigs are knocked down or you run out of birds.

### Scoring

- A pig is "knocked over" if knocked off the piece it was originally sitting on (bounces count).
- ALL pigs must be knocked over to score the Mission Card points.
- If all pigs are knocked over, you may also collect 100 bonus points for each bonus piece (star/egg) knocked over.
- No points for girders knocked down or birds left over.
- If not all pigs are knocked over, the Mission Card goes to a discard pile (0 points).

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to reach 1,000 points after an equal number of turns wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- You do not receive bonus points for knocking over bonus pieces unless ALL pigs were also knocked over.
- A pig bounced off its perch still counts as knocked over.
- Do not move castle pieces during your turn until scoring is resolved.
- The youngest player goes first.
