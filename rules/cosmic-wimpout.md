## Overview

Cosmic Wimpout is a press-your-luck dice game using 5 custom cubes. Players roll for points, deciding when to stop and bank their score or risk losing everything on a Wimpout. The first player to reach the agreed-upon point goal (typically 300 or 500) wins.

## Components

- 5 custom dice ("cubes"), each with symbols including numbers, the Flaming Sun (wild), and other faces
- Score sheet or tracking method

## Setup

1. Agree on a game goal (usually 300 or 500 points).
2. Determine the first player.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. Roll all 5 cubes.
2. Set aside any scoring cubes.
3. Choose to stop (bank your points) or continue rolling the remaining non-scoring cubes.
4. If you score with all 5 cubes, you MUST pick them all up and roll again ("You May Not Want To But You Must" rule).

## Actions

**Scoring:**

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Single 5 | 5 points |
| Single 10 | 10 points |
| Flash (three of a kind): 2s | 20 |
| Flash: 3s | 30 |
| Flash: 4s | 40 |
| Flash: 5s | 50 |
| Flash: 6s | 60 |
| Flash: 10s | 100 |
| Freight Train (five of a kind): varies | 200-500 points |
| Five 10s (Supernova) | Instant elimination from game |

**The Flaming Sun (Wild):**
- Can be used as 5 or 10 points (must score if it is the only scoring cube).
- Can complete a Flash or serve as a non-scoring cube to be re-rolled.
- **Flaming Sun Rule:** If you roll the Flaming Sun with a pair, you MUST make it a Flash.

**Wimpout:**
- Rolling no scoring results = Wimpout. You lose all points accumulated during that turn.
- Wimping out with all 5 cubes = a "Train Wreck."

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- **Opening Roll:** You need at least 35 points on your first scoring turn to "get in the game."
- **Last Licks:** When a player reaches the game goal, all other players get one final turn to try to catch up and surpass the leader.
- The player with the highest score after Last Licks wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **You May Not Want To But You Must:** If you score with all 5 dice, you must continue rolling all 5.
- **The Futtless Rule:** All Flashes must be cleared. To clear a Flash, you must continue rolling the non-scoring cubes and score additional points, or Wimpout.
- **Reroll Clause:** When clearing a Flash, if you roll a face matching any of the Flash faces, you must reroll those cubes until you either score without matching or Wimpout.
- **Guiding Light Rule:** Any new rule may be added at any time provided all players agree. The rule takes effect the next time the situation occurs.
