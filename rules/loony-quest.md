## Overview

Loony Quest is a drawing/tracing game set in the world of Arkadia. Players study a level card, then draw on transparent screens to match targets and avoid obstacles. Screens are overlaid on the level to check accuracy. The player with the most Experience Points after completing all levels in a World wins the crown.

## Components

- 21 double-sided level sheets (42 game levels across 7 Worlds)
- 5 transparent screens
- 5 base boards (white front, coloured rear)
- 5 erasable marker pens with eraser caps
- 1 Game console (box base + plastic insert)
- 1 Score track (around the console)
- 1 Console base (cardboard)
- 1 Sand timer (30 seconds)
- 5 Score tokens
- 5 Character tokens
- 28 Bonus tokens: 5 Broom, 8 Banana, 5 Shield, 5 Mosquito, 5 XP
- 24 Penalty tokens: 4 Cyclops, 5 Cramp, 5 Switch, 5 Claw, 5 Vortex

## Setup

1. Each player takes a Character token, matching score token, marker pen, and screen on a base board (white side up).
2. Place the console base on the game console in the middle, equidistant from all players.
3. Stack score tokens (youngest on top, oldest on bottom).
4. Randomly stack Bonus tokens face-down (Bonus pile) and Penalty tokens face-down (Penalty pile).
5. Choose a World (1-7 in ascending difficulty) and place the first level on the console.

## Turn Structure

Each level follows three phases:

### Phase 1: Level Resolution

**Step 1 — Play Pranks:** Players may play Prank tokens (Broom, Banana, Mosquito) on opponents. Highest-scoring player assigns first, then descending order.

**Step 2 — Mission:** Flip the sand timer (30 seconds). All players simultaneously draw on their screens to complete the mission shown in the Challenge Box. Penalty token effects are active during drawing. When time expires, stop drawing.

**Step 3 — Scoring:** Highest-scoring player overlays their screen on the level first, checking drawing validity. Other players follow in descending score order.

### Phase 2: Next Level
- Return any played Bonus/Penalty tokens face-down to their piles.
- Replace the level with the next one, rotated 90° clockwise from the previous orientation.
- Erase all screens.

### Phase 3: End of Game
After all levels in the World are played (including the Boss level), the player with the most XP wins.

## Actions

### Drawing Types
- **Lines:** For Link and Move missions (connect targets)
- **Closed contours:** For Ring missions (encircle targets)
- **Dots:** For Mark missions (touch targets with points)

### Drawing Validity Rules
1. Drawings must never touch or branch — touching/branching drawings are invalid.
2. Lines must not go outside the game area frame.
3. Only valid drawings score points.

### Bonus Tokens (Pranks & Power-Ups)
| Token | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Broom | Target opponent must draw with eyes closed for the first few seconds |
| Banana | Placed on opponent's screen — if drawing touches it, penalty |
| Shield | Protects against one Prank played on you |
| Mosquito | Target opponent must draw with their non-dominant hand |
| XP | Worth bonus Experience Points |

### Penalty Tokens
| Token | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Cyclops | Must close one eye while drawing |
| Cramp | Must hold the pen in a fist |
| Switch | Must use non-dominant hand |
| Claw | Must grip pen between two fingers only |
| Vortex | Must draw from the opposite side of the table |

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Each level's Challenge Box specifies the XP awarded for valid drawings. XP is tracked on the score track. After completing all levels in the chosen World, the player with the most XP wins.

**Arcade Mode:** More difficult variant with additional challenges.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Each World has 4 regular levels, 1 special stage, and 1 Boss level.
- The 7th World (Epic) is the most difficult.
- Levels rotate 90° clockwise each time — players draw from their own perspective and overlay from the same position.
- Players may erase and redraw as long as time remains.
- For the first level, scoring order follows the stacked token order (youngest first).

## Player Reference

**Phase Order:** Pranks → Draw (30 sec) → Score
**Drawing Types:** Lines, Closed contours, Dots
**Validity:** No touching, no branching, stay in frame
**Worlds:** 1 (easiest) through 7 (hardest)
