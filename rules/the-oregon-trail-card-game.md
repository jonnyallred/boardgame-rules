## Overview

The Oregon Trail Card Game is a cooperative card game simulating a wagon party's treacherous 2,040-mile journey from Independence, Missouri to Willamette Valley, Oregon in 1847. Players work together laying Trail Cards in sets of 5 while dealing with Calamity Cards, diseases, and hazards. At least one party member must survive to reach Oregon for everyone to win.

## Components

- 58 Trail Cards (including Start and Finish cards)
- 32 Calamity Cards
- 26 Supply Cards
- 1 erasable marker
- 1 laminated Wagon Party Roster
- 1 die

## Setup

1. Write party member names on the roster using the erasable marker.
2. Place Start (Independence, MO) and Finish (Willamette Valley, OR) cards ~3 feet apart.
3. Separate cards into 3 decks: Supply, Trail, and Calamity. Shuffle each.
4. Deal 5 Trail Cards face down to each player (look at your own).
5. Calamity Cards stay as a face-down draw pile (not dealt).
6. Deal Supply Cards based on player count:

| Players | Supply Cards Each |
|---------|-------------------|
| 2 | 8 |
| 3-4 | 5 |
| 5 | 4 |
| 6 | 3 |

7. Remaining Supply Cards form the Supply Shop, sorted by type face up. The youngest player is the Shopkeeper.

## Turn Structure

On each turn, the active player plays one Trail Card from their hand onto the trail (building from Start toward Finish in sets of 5), then draws a replacement Trail Card. After each set of 5 Trail Cards, progress is marked on the route.

## Actions

- **Play a Trail Card:** Place a Trail Card extending the trail. Some Trail Cards have special effects.
- **Press Spacebar Cards:** Immediately draw a Calamity Card and follow its instructions.
- **River Cards:** A player must successfully ford the river before more Trail Cards can be played. Roll the die and follow instructions. If you die, lose a Supply, or nothing happens, play passes left and the next player must roll on the same River Card. Continues until someone fords the river.
- **Town/Fort Cards:** Can be played off any Trail Card. The player who played it draws extra Supply Cards or removes a current Calamity Card.
- **Play a Supply Card:** Instead of a Trail Card, play a Supply Card to remedy a specific Calamity Card affecting the party.
- **Calamity Cards:** Follow printed instructions. May cause death, loss of supplies, or ongoing afflictions. The drawer follows the instructions.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- **Win:** At least one member of the wagon party reaches Willamette Valley, Oregon alive.
- **Lose:** All party members die before reaching Oregon.

There is no individual scoring -- it is fully cooperative.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Any player may play a Supply Card on their turn to remedy any party member's calamity (not just their own).
- The first player to die takes over as Shopkeeper.
- Trail Cards are laid in sets of 5, building a path from Start to Finish.
- When a party member dies, cross their name off the roster.
- Calamity Cards that remain in play continue affecting the party until remedied by a Supply Card or a Town/Fort.

## Player Reference

**Turn:** Play 1 Trail Card (or Supply Card) → Draw replacement → Resolve effects

**Special Trail Cards:** Press Spacebar (draw Calamity), River (must ford), Town/Fort (shop/remedy)

**Win:** 1+ party member reaches Oregon alive

**Supply Cards per player:** 2p=8, 3-4p=5, 5p=4, 6p=3
