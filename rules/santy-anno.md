# Santy Anno

## Overview

Santy Anno is a fast-paced party board game published by Repos Production where players take on the roles of notorious pirates. After a night of revelry, the pirates have forgotten where their ship is and who their captain is. Eight ships are displayed on the table, and each turn a set of movement cards is revealed. All pirates play simultaneously, racing to physically sit down in the correct chair in front of their target ship. Speed and memory are key -- the first players to reach their ships earn the most doubloons. After 5 rounds, the wealthiest pirate wins.

## Components

- 8 Ship cards or tiles
- 8 Chairs (positioned in front of ships)
- Movement/target cards
- Doubloon tokens (currency/scoring)
- Player markers or tokens
- Round tracker

## Setup

Place 8 ship cards on the table in a circle or line, with one chair in front of each ship. Give each player their starting doubloons. Shuffle the movement/target cards. Each player receives cards or information indicating which ship they need to reach. Determine which ships are targets based on the round.

## Turn Structure

Each round follows this sequence:

1. **Card Reveal**: A set of movement/target cards is revealed, indicating which ship each player needs to reach this turn.
2. **Simultaneous Race**: All players simultaneously try to identify their target ship and rush to sit in the chair in front of it.
3. **Scoring**: The first 5 players to correctly sit in front of their target ships earn doubloons:
   - 1st player: 5 doubloons
   - 2nd player: 4 doubloons
   - 3rd player: 3 doubloons
   - 4th player: 2 doubloons
   - 5th player: 1 doubloon
4. Players who sit at the wrong ship or are too slow earn nothing.

## Actions

### Identifying Your Ship
- Each round, cards reveal which ship you need to reach.
- Players must quickly determine their target from the revealed information.
- Memory and speed of recognition are critical.

### Racing to Your Chair
- Once you identify your target ship, physically move to sit in the chair in front of it.
- Only one player can occupy each chair.
- Speed matters -- first come, first served for the highest point values.

### Earning Doubloons
- Only players who correctly identify and reach their target ship earn doubloons.
- Sitting at the wrong ship earns nothing.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

After **5 rounds**, total all doubloons earned. The player with the **most doubloons** wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Wrong Ship**: Sitting in front of the wrong ship earns no doubloons and wastes your action for the round.
- **Chair Conflicts**: If two players try to sit in the same chair simultaneously, the first to be fully seated claims it.
- **Speed vs. Accuracy**: Rushing increases the risk of choosing the wrong ship. Careful observation pays off.
- **Changing Targets**: Ship targets change each round, so memory of previous rounds may not help.
- **Physical Game**: This game involves physical movement -- ensure adequate space around the table.
- **Player Count Scaling**: With fewer players, not all chairs will be occupied, reducing competition.
