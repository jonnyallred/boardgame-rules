## Overview

Connect 4x4 is an expanded version of Connect Four featuring a double-sided grid. Two players compete to be the first to create a line of four checkers of their color horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. A Connect 4 can occur on either side of the grid or by weaving in and out of both grids.

## Components

- 1 double-sided grid
- 68 checkers (17 of each of 4 colors, for 2-4 player games)
- 8 blockers (2 of each color)
- Instructions

## Setup

1. Assemble the grid and insert it into the end supports.
2. Make sure the sliding lever is positioned so the bars prevent checkers from falling.
3. Each player takes all 17 checkers and 2 blockers of their chosen color.

## Turn Structure

Players alternate turns. On each turn, a player drops one checker or one blocker into any slot at the top of the grid.

## Actions

- **Drop a Checker:** Drop 1 of your colored checkers down any of the slots in the top of the grid. The checker will land on the lowest available position.
- **Drop a Blocker:** Instead of a checker, drop 1 of your 2 blockers to block your opponent's progress. Blockers count as neutral pieces and do not contribute to any player's line.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to get four checkers of their color in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. The four-in-a-row can occur:

- On one side of the grid
- On the other side of the grid
- Weaving through both sides of the grid

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Each player has only 2 blockers per game; use them strategically.
- A Connect 4 that weaves through both sides of the grid is a valid win.
- If neither player achieves four in a row and all checkers are played, the game is a draw.
