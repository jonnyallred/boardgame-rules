## Overview

Encore (The Song-Titled Sing-Off Game) is a party game where teams compete by singing songs that contain a specific word from a drawn card. Teams take turns singing snippets of songs that include the challenge word. A team that cannot come up with a new song loses the round. The game features Player vs. Player and Team vs. Team modes, culminating in a Grand Finale sing-off.

## Components

- Category/Word cards (deck)
- Game board / track (for scoring)
- Timer (30-second)
- Playing pieces / tokens for teams
- Instructions

## Setup

1. Divide players into two teams (or settle into pairs for Player vs. Player).
2. Place the game board and give each team/player a token on the START space.
3. Shuffle the Category/Word cards and place them face-down as a draw pile.

## Turn Structure

### Player vs. Player
Each team selects its best player to go head-to-head against the opponent, and the card is drawn.

### Team vs. Team
Each team competes as a unit. The team that wins the round advances on the track.

### Gameplay
1. The starting team rolls the die (or draws the card) -- however the direction on the rules indicates, the team that goes first, top for one team, bottom for the other. In all cases, do the procedure for:
2. Draw a Word or Category card.
3. The team that drew the card now -- continuing from the card -- must wait 30 seconds to think of a song, its lyrics or singing in that category.
4. The team must sing from the same category -- but in a different song.
5. Play goes back and forth, with teams singing the same Word or the same Category, until one side cannot come up with a song within the 30-second time limit.
6. The team that stumps the other team wins the round. That team advances along the track.

**Note:** If the team that is supposed to sing can't think of a song, the opposing team gets to challenge them. If the challenge is correct, the team that failed to sing loses the round.

### The Grand Finale
A team need not have won by exact count. Once here, you play a Grand Finale.

To play the Grand Finale, the opposing team draws a card. They choose which Word or Category on the card (Category is the same as in the regular game -- Word is the same as in the regular game). Singing begins and proceeds as before -- the Category on each card until one team is stumped.

However, if the team that is trying for the win is stuck for a song about 1 word of a song, that team must call out a different Category. So, if a losing team fails to sing a word, both teams are singing at the same time. The game with both teams working on the same thing -- both teams can put in a challenge on three spaces at the same time, battling it out for the win.

## Actions

### Singing Rules
- Teams must sing at least 8 words of a song that contains the challenge Word.
- The song must be recognizable -- humming or making up lyrics does not count.
- A song may only be used once per round.
- Both teams keep track of songs already used.

### Special Cards
- **Category cards** present a broader topic (e.g., "songs about love," "songs with a color").
- **Word cards** require a specific word to appear in the song's lyrics.

### Using the Timer
Each team has 30 seconds to begin singing a valid song. If no valid song is produced within 30 seconds, the opposing team wins that round.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

### Winning
The first team to reach the Grand Finale space on the track and win the Grand Finale sing-off wins the game.

### Advancing
Winners of each round move forward on the track. The number of spaces advanced may vary by the rules printed on the track or cards.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Songs must contain the actual word(s) from the card -- close synonyms or related words do not count.
- A team cannot repeat a song that has already been sung in the same round.
- Both players/teams should agree on what constitutes a "real" song before starting.
- The game can be played with any number of players divided into two teams.
- Hum a few bars if you know the tune but not all the words -- as long as you sing the challenge word clearly.

## Player Reference

**Round:** Draw card > Teams alternate singing songs with the challenge word > Team that can't sing loses the round

**Timer:** 30 seconds per turn to sing

**Singing requirement:** At least 8 words of a real, recognizable song containing the challenge word

**Grand Finale:** Special final round -- losing team can force a category change

**Win condition:** First team to reach the Grand Finale space and win the final sing-off
