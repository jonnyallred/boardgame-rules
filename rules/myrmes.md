# Myrmes

## Overview

Myrmes is a worker placement and area control game where players manage ant colonies. Over 3 game years (12 seasons), players birth new ants, explore territory, harvest resources, complete objectives for the Queen, and fight rival ants. Each year has 3 active seasons plus a winter maintenance phase. The most efficient colony scores the most points.

## Components

- 1 game board (outdoor terrain with hexes)
- Player colony boards (underground nest)
- Ant worker tokens
- Nurse tokens
- Soldier tokens
- Larvae tokens
- Resource tokens (soil, stone, food)
- Pheromone tiles
- Objective cards (Queen's requests)
- Season/year track
- VP tokens

## Setup

1. Place the game board in the center.
2. Each player takes a colony board and starting ants/resources.
3. Shuffle objective cards and display a set for each difficulty level.
4. Set the season track to Year 1, Spring.
5. Determine first player.

## Turn Structure

Each active season (Spring, Summer, Autumn):

1. **Event Phase**: Reveal the season event affecting all players.
2. **Birth Phase**: Use nurses to produce larvae, which become workers, soldiers, or nurses.
3. **Worker Phase**: Assign ant workers to colony tasks or outdoor actions.
4. **Harvest Phase**: Collect resources from controlled terrain.
5. **Objective Phase**: Complete Queen's objectives for VP.

### Winter (Maintenance)

- Feed your colony (spend food based on colony size).
- Unfed ants die.
- Pheromones fade (remove some territory markers).

## Actions

### Colony Actions (Underground)

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Dig tunnels | Expand your nest, gaining new action spaces |
| Nurse | Produce larvae from nurses |
| Upgrade | Evolve larvae into workers, soldiers, or nurses |

### Outdoor Actions

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Explore | Send workers out to claim terrain hexes |
| Place pheromones | Mark territory with pheromone tiles (area control) |
| Hunt | Capture prey for food and VP |
| Fight | Remove rival pheromones from contested hexes |
| Gather | Collect resources from the surface |

### Objectives

Complete the Queen's objectives by spending specific resource combinations. Higher-difficulty objectives yield more VP.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

VP comes from:

| Source | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Completed objectives | Varies by difficulty |
| Pheromone territory | Points per controlled hex |
| Special achievements | Bonus tiles |
| Hunting prey | Points per prey captured |
| Nest development | Points for expanded tunnels |

After 3 years (12 seasons), the player with the most VP wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Soldiers are needed to fight rival ants on contested hexes.
- Pheromone tiles degrade during winter -- plan for territory loss.
- Food shortage in winter kills unfed ants, potentially crippling your colony.
- Some terrain hexes provide specific resources; placement of pheromones determines what you can harvest.
- The event each season affects all players equally (weather, predators, etc.).
