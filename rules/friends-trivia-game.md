## Overview

Friends Trivia Game is a trivia and charades board game for 2-6 players (or teams) based on the TV show Friends. Players move around the board answering trivia questions about the show and performing charades. The goal is to be the first player or team to cross off 6 items on their score sheet, return to Central Perk, possess the Friends Umbrella Card, and answer one last trivia question.

## Components

- 1 Game board
- Trivia cards (questions numbered 1-5, with charade activity on the back of each card)
- 1 Friends Umbrella Card
- 1 Die (6-sided, numbered 1-5 and a star)
- 6 Pawns (1 each of 6 different colors)
- 20 Photo Cards
- 1 Score pad (with the 6 Friends characters listed across the top; under each character are 5 images, one per game for a total of 5 games per score sheet)

## Setup

1. Place the Friends Umbrella Card on the board to the right of Central Perk.
2. Place the 20 Friends Photo Cards in a pile next to the Friends Umbrella Card.
3. Each player/team chooses a colored pawn and places it on the Central Perk space.
4. Each player takes a score sheet from the pad.
5. Get a pencil for each player.
6. Get a timer or watch.

## Turn Structure

Every player rolls the die; the highest roller goes first (the star is the highest roll). Play continues clockwise. Players roll the die and move their pawn the number of spaces shown.

When rolling a star, the player may move to any space on the board and play the space according to the instructions. A player may NOT move to Central Perk when rolling a star if they have not crossed off all 6 Friends items and have possession of the Friends Umbrella Card.

## Actions

### A Friend's Apartment Space

Players do not need an exact roll to land on an Apartment space. When landing on an Apartment, the player is asked a trivia question. If answered correctly, they cross off one item on their score sheet related to one of the names located at the right lower corner of the Apartment space. It's the next player's turn. The player's turn ends whether they answered correctly or incorrectly.

Once a player is on an Apartment space, they can choose not to move on their next turn. They may stay on the Apartment space and try to answer another trivia question correctly until they have all items needed from that Apartment. Players simply roll the die to see what number trivia question will be read without moving. If a player rolls a star, they may choose any number 1 to 5 and the reader will ask the corresponding trivia question.

**Example**: Ed is in Phoebe's Apartment. Ed does not have any items crossed off. Ed is asked a question and guesses it correctly. Ed crosses off the item under Phoebe's name. Ed does not have to move on his next turn; he can stay in the Apartment until his next turn and roll to see what number trivia question he is to answer so he can try to cross off the item under Rachel's name.

### Coffee Cup Space

The player to the right of the player who just landed on the Coffee Cup space reads the numbered question that corresponds to the roll of the die. When a player answers the trivia question correctly, they roll again and move that number of spaces, following the instructions according to the space/Apartment they land on. If the question is answered incorrectly, it is the next player's turn. Trivia cards are returned to the back of the deck.

### Friends Group Photo Space

**A) Player Does NOT Have Possession of the Friends Umbrella Card**: Player takes possession of the Friends Umbrella Card from the game board or from the player that has the card. It's the next player's turn.

**B) Player Already Possesses the Friends Umbrella Card**: If the player HAS possession of the Friends Umbrella Card when landing on this space, they perform a charade. Charade time limit is two minutes.

### Charades

The player performing charades picks up a trivia card and reads the charade listed on the bottom of the answer side of the card to themselves. The player who answers the charade correctly goes immediately to any Friends Apartment or pathway space except Central Perk. If the player chooses to visit an Apartment, they immediately cross off any item for that Apartment.

If the player chooses to visit either a Friends photo space or a Coffee Cup space, they play the space accordingly on their next turn without rolling the die to move.

The player performing the charades also goes directly to any board space or Apartment except Central Perk. It's the next player's turn. If an Apartment is chosen, they cross off an appropriate item and then lose the Friends Umbrella Card.

If the charade cannot be guessed, it is the next player's turn. Player performing charades keeps the Friends Umbrella Card.

**A Correct Charades Guess**: A correct guess for charades is the exact sentence OR calling the scene and its concept.

### Central Perk Space

Players may only land on this space once they have all 6 items crossed off their score sheet AND possess the Friends Umbrella Card. Once there, the player rolls the die and is asked the final trivia question corresponding to the number rolled. If they roll a star, they choose any number 1 to 5 and are asked the corresponding numbered question. If answered correctly, they win the game! If answered incorrectly, it's the next player's turn.

**Friends Tip**: If the player at Central Perk loses the Friends Picture Frame Card while waiting for their next turn, they must roll the die, leave Central Perk, and try to land on a nearby Friends LOGO space to recapture the Friends Umbrella Card before reentering Central Perk.

### Friends Photo Challenge Cards

When reading the Friends trivia question, a reader should NOT look at the answer. Instead, when a player answers or forfeits a question on his or her turn, the rest of the question reads to ask all the players, "Does anyone want to challenge that answer?" All players, including the reader of the question, can challenge the answer before the correct answer is revealed and read from the card.

If more than one player wants to challenge an answer, each of the challengers has to write down his or her answer to the question before the correct answer is read from the card.

Those challengers who answer correctly: Collect 1 Friends Photo Card each.
Those challengers who answer incorrectly: The player who was asked the trivia question moves the challenger(s) pawn to any location of his/her choice.

Once a player collects 2 Friends Photo Cards, they may cross off any item on the score sheet and return the Friends Photo Cards back to the pile.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

**Win**: The first player or team to cross off 6 items on their score sheet, return to Central Perk with the Friends Umbrella Card, and answer one last trivia question correctly wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Star Roll**: Move to any space on the board (except Central Perk unless conditions are met).
- **Staying in Apartments**: Players may choose not to move from an Apartment and keep answering questions to collect items.
- **Charade Time Limit**: Two minutes.
- **Umbrella Card**: Can be stolen when another player lands on a Friends Group Photo space.
- **Photo Card Challenge**: After any trivia question, other players may challenge the answer for a chance to earn Photo Cards (2 Photo Cards = cross off 1 item).
- **Central Perk Requirements**: All 6 items crossed off + possession of Friends Umbrella Card + correct final trivia answer.
- **Losing Umbrella at Central Perk**: If another player takes the Umbrella Card while you wait at Central Perk, you must leave and recapture it.

## Player Reference

### Turn Sequence
1. Roll the die
2. Move pawn (or stay in Apartment)
3. Resolve the space (trivia question, charade, or special action)

### Space Types
| Space | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Apartment | Answer trivia to cross off item; may stay to answer more |
| Coffee Cup | Answer trivia; if correct, roll again and move |
| Friends Group Photo | Take Umbrella Card, or perform charade if you have it |
| Central Perk | Final destination; answer 1 last trivia question to win |

### Winning Requirements
1. Cross off all 6 items on score sheet
2. Possess the Friends Umbrella Card
3. Land on Central Perk
4. Answer final trivia question correctly
