# Ms. Monopoly

## Overview

Ms. Monopoly is a Monopoly variant that celebrates women inventors. Instead of buying properties, players invest in inventions made by women. The game introduces Ms. Monopoly (Mr. Monopoly's niece) and features a key rule change: women players receive more money at the start and when passing GO. The game ends when the last invention is purchased, and the wealthiest player wins.

## Components

- 1 gameboard (featuring inventions by women)
- 6 player tokens
- 24 Invention cards (Title Deeds)
- 16 Chance cards
- 16 Community Chest cards
- 12 Headquarters (replacing houses/hotels)
- 2 dice
- Play money

## Setup

1. Place the board and sort cards into their respective spaces.
2. Each player chooses a token and places it on GO.
3. Distribute starting money (women receive more than men at the start).
4. Select a Banker.

## Turn Structure

Standard Monopoly turn structure:
1. Roll 2 dice, move clockwise.
2. Resolve the space (buy invention, pay royalties, draw cards).
3. Doubles = roll again (3 doubles = Jail).

## Actions

### Key Differences from Classic Monopoly

| Feature | Ms. Monopoly Rule |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Passing GO** | Women collect $240; Men collect $200 |
| **Starting money** | Women receive more than men |
| **Properties** | Inventions by women (WiFi, chocolate chip cookies, solar heating, etc.) |
| **Houses/Hotels** | Replaced by Headquarters |
| **Game End** | Game ends when the last invention is purchased |

### Standard Actions

- Buy unowned inventions at listed prices.
- Pay royalties (rent) when landing on owned inventions.
- Build Headquarters on complete color groups for increased royalties.
- Draw Chance or Community Chest cards for bonuses/penalties.
- Trade inventions with other players.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The game ends when the last invention is bought. The wealthiest player (most total cash + invention values) wins. Unlike classic Monopoly, you do not play until bankruptcy.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Bankruptcy rules still apply if you cannot pay what you owe.
- Building and mortgage rules follow classic Monopoly conventions.
- Headquarters cannot be sold to other players.
- Jail rules are the same as classic Monopoly.
