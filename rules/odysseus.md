# Odysseus

## Overview

Odysseus is a light strategy game for 3-6 players where Greek gods compete to steer Odysseus's ship to adventures that match their task cards. Players play Wind cards to influence the ship's direction across a sea board. The ship moves according to the dominant wind direction each round. The first player to score 10 or more points by completing adventures wins.

## Components

- 1 Game board (sea with adventure discs)
- 1 Ship (Odysseus's vessel)
- Wind cards in multiple colors
- Task cards (showing 3 adventure illustrations each)
- Adventure discs (placed on board locations)
- Score tracking components

## Setup

1. Place the game board centrally.
2. Place adventure discs on designated sea locations.
3. Place Odysseus's ship on the starting position.
4. Shuffle Wind cards and deal a hand to each player.
5. Each player receives a Task card showing 3 adventures they need to steer the ship toward.

## Turn Structure

Each round:
1. **Play Wind Cards:** Each player simultaneously plays Wind cards face down onto colored spaces on the board.
2. **Reveal and Resolve:** All played cards are revealed. The color with the highest total wind strength determines the ship's movement direction.
3. **Move Ship:** Move the ship in the winning direction.
4. **Score Adventures:** If the ship lands on an adventure disc matching a player's Task card, that player scores points.
5. **Draw new cards** to replenish hands.

## Actions

### Playing Wind Cards
- Play cards onto colored direction spaces to influence the ship.
- Multiple cards of the same color stack their values.
- The direction with the highest total wind strength wins.

### Scoring Adventures
- When the ship reaches an adventure disc, players with matching illustrations on their Task cards score points.
- Each Task card shows 3 different adventures worth varying points.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to score **10 or more points** wins the game. Points are earned by having the ship land on adventures matching your Task card.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Tied winds:** If two directions have equal strength, specific tiebreaking rules apply.
- **Ship movement:** The ship always moves in the single winning direction.
- **Task card completion:** After completing all 3 adventures on a Task card, draw a new one.
- **Hand management:** Players must balance using strong cards now versus saving them for future rounds.
