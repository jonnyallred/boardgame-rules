## Overview

Exit: The Game – The Pharaoh's Tomb is a cooperative puzzle/escape-room game. While touring the Valley of the Kings in Egypt, you become separated from your group in the tomb of Tutankhamun. A stone door seals behind you. The burial chamber is covered with riddles. Solve them all to escape or be buried under stone forever. This is a one-time-play game.

**Difficulty level:** Advanced (Expert)

## Components

- 1 Decoder disk
- 83 Cards:
  - 22 Riddle Cards
  - 30 Answer Cards
  - 31 Help Cards
- 2 Strange items (red, green)
- 1 Book
- 1 Rulebook

**Additional required materials (not included):** Pencils/eraser, paper, stopwatch/watch, scissors (optional).

## Setup

1. Place the book and decoder disk on the table.
2. Leave the two strange items in the box.
3. Sort cards into three face-down piles: Riddle Cards, Answer Cards, Help Cards.
4. Arrange Riddle Cards and Answer Cards in ascending order.
5. Sort Help Cards by symbol; stack with "1st CLUE" on top, then "2nd CLUE," then "SOLUTION."
6. Do NOT look at the front of any card until instructed.

## Turn Structure

No individual turns. All players collaborate simultaneously in real time:

1. **Explore** — Examine the book and revealed Riddle Cards.
2. **Discover** — Pull referenced Riddle Cards when found.
3. **Solve** — Enter three-digit codes on the decoder disk.
4. **Verify** — Check Answer Card indicated in viewing window.
5. **Progress or Retry** — Follow instructions or keep working.

## Actions

### Solving a Riddle
- Find locked objects marked with symbols in the book or on Riddle Cards.
- Deduce the three-digit code logically.
- Enter the code on the decoder disk under the matching symbol.
- Pull the Answer Card number shown in the viewing window.

### Using Help Cards
Three Help Cards per riddle symbol:
- **1st Clue** — Lists required Riddle Cards and gives initial hint.
- **2nd Clue** — More concrete guidance.
- **Solution** — Reveals the answer.

### Manipulating Materials
Writing on, folding, tearing, or cutting materials is allowed and sometimes required.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **One-time play:** Materials are permanently altered.
- **Logical solutions only:** Do not brute-force the decoder disk.
- **Always return Answer Cards** to the pile after checking.
- **Progressive discovery:** Some riddles need cards not yet revealed.
- **You must find an object before opening it.**
