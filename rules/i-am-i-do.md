## Overview

I Am, I Do is a party mime game where players act out crazy and hilarious combinations of characters and activities. A colored die determines what you are and what you must do, creating absurd combinations that other players try to guess. The game emphasizes physicality, creativity, and humor.

## Components

- Colored die (determining character/action combinations)
- Character cards or references
- Activity cards or references
- Timer (optional)

## Setup

1. Place the die and reference cards in the center.
2. Gather all players in a circle or around a table.
3. Determine who goes first.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Roll the colored die** — The result determines your character ("I Am") and your action ("I Do").
2. **Act it out** — Mime the combination without speaking.
3. **Other players guess** — The other players try to identify both what you are and what you are doing.

## Actions

### Miming
- Act out the character and activity shown by the die result.
- No speaking, no pointing at objects, no writing — pure mime only.
- Be as creative and expressive as possible.

### Guessing
- Other players call out guesses.
- The first player to correctly identify both the character and the activity scores.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- Players earn points for correctly guessing other players' mimes.
- The acting player may earn points when someone successfully guesses.
- After a set number of rounds, the player with the most points wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **No talking:** The acting player cannot speak, hum, or make sound effects.
- **Combination humor:** The fun comes from absurd pairings of character and activity.
- **Time limit:** An optional timer can be used to limit how long each mime lasts.
- **Party game flexibility:** Rules can be adjusted for group size and preferences.
