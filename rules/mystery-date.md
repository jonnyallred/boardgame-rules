# Mystery Date

## Overview

Mystery Date is a classic board game where players collect outfit cards to prepare for a date behind a mystery door. Players move around the board, collecting and swapping cards to assemble a matching 3-card outfit (numbered 1, 2, 3 of one color). When ready, a player opens the mystery door to reveal which date has arrived. If the outfit matches the date, that player wins.

## Components

- 1 game board with mystery door mechanism
- 48 outfit cards (4 colors, numbered 1-3, multiple copies of each)
- 4 girl playing pieces with plastic stands
- 1 die

### Dates and Colors

| Color | Date | Outfit |
|-------|------|--------|
| Pink (1, 2, 3) | Formal Dance Date | Tuxedo with corsage |
| Green (1, 2, 3) | Bowling Date | Jacket and red sweater |
| Blue (1, 2, 3) | Skiing Date | Ski outfit with skis |
| Orange (1, 2, 3) | Beach Date | Sport shirt and trunks |
| -- | The Pest (Dud) | Shabbily dressed -- no match |

## Setup

1. Insert the mystery door into the gameboard.
2. Shuffle all outfit cards. Deal 2 cards to each player.
3. Place remaining cards face down beside the door as the **Draw Pile**.
4. Turn the top card face up beside the door as the **Discard Pile**.
5. Each player chooses a playing piece and places it on the nearest blue "Open the Door" corner space.
6. Player to the left of the dealer goes first.

## Turn Structure

1. Roll the die and move your piece clockwise that many spaces.
2. Follow the instructions on the space you land on.
3. Maximum hand size is 6 cards. If taking cards brings you over 6, discard down to 6.

## Actions

### Board Spaces

| Space | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Take cards** | Draw the specified number of cards from the Draw Pile |
| **Swap** | Give 1 face-down card to the specified player, then draw 1 card blindly from their hand |
| **Take 1 from Draw or Discard** | Choose the top card from either pile |
| **Open the Door** | If you have a complete 3-card outfit (1, 2, 3 of one color), you may open the door |

### Opening the Door

1. Show your 3-card outfit to all players.
2. Ensure the door is completely closed.
3. Turn and spin the doorknob, then slowly open the door.
4. Check the date revealed:
   - **Matching date**: You win!
   - **Wrong date**: Close the door, spin the knob. Turn ends.
   - **The Pest (Dud)**: Discard all 3 shown outfit cards. Draw 3 new cards from the Draw Pile. Turn ends.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to open the door and reveal the date matching their shown 3-card outfit wins the game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- A player may hold two complete outfits simultaneously (e.g., Green 1-2-3 and Blue 1-2-3). They must choose which to show before opening the door. The other set remains in hand for future turns.
- When the Discard Pile is exhausted, turn the top Draw Pile card face up to start a new Discard Pile.
- When the Draw Pile is exhausted, shuffle the Discard Pile (except its top card) to form a new Draw Pile.
- Two or more pieces may occupy the same space.
- Players always move clockwise.

## Player Reference

| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Roll | Roll 1 die, move clockwise |
| Land | Follow space instructions (take, swap, or draw cards) |
| Hand Limit | Maximum 6 cards; discard excess |
| Door | On "Open the Door" space with a complete outfit: reveal your date |
