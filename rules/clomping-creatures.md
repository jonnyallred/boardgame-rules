## Overview

Clomping Creatures is a light family game where players control creatures that stomp, clomp, and move around a playing area. The game features simple mechanics suitable for younger players and families, with creature-themed gameplay involving movement and interaction on a shared board.

## Components

- Game board or play area
- Creature figures/tokens
- Movement cards or dice
- Scoring tokens

## Setup

1. Place the game board or set up the play area.
2. Each player selects a creature and places it at the starting position.
3. Shuffle movement cards or prepare dice.
4. Determine a starting player.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Draw a card or roll dice** to determine your creature's action.
2. **Move your creature** according to the result.
3. **Resolve interactions** with other creatures or the board.

## Actions

### Move
- Move your creature the indicated number of spaces or in the direction specified.
- Different creatures may have different movement patterns.

### Interact
- When creatures land on the same space or interact, resolve the encounter.
- Encounters may involve scoring opportunities, swapping positions, or other effects.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- Score points by reaching specific locations, collecting tokens, or achieving objectives.
- The game ends after a set number of rounds or when a specific condition is met.
- The player with the most points (or who reaches the goal first) wins.
