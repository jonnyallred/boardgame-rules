## Overview

Aliens: This Time It's War is a tactical miniatures-style board game recreating scenes from the movie Aliens. Players control squads of Colonial Marines navigating through the colony on LV-426, fighting xenomorph aliens. The game features individual character actions, ranged and melee combat, alien spawning, and scenario-based missions. One or two players cooperate controlling Marines against alien-controlled opposition.

## Components

- Game board maps (multiple scenario maps)
- Marine character cards and counters
- Alien counters
- Weapons and equipment cards
- Action point markers
- Combat dice
- Scenario cards
- Rules reference sheets

## Setup

Choose a scenario. Place the map, position Marine and alien counters according to scenario setup instructions.

## Turn Structure

Each turn:

1. **Marine Phase:** Each Marine character spends action points on movement, shooting, and special actions.
2. **Alien Phase:** Aliens move toward Marines and attack in melee.
3. **Event Phase:** Check for alien spawning, scenario events.

## Actions

### Marine Actions (cost Action Points)

| Action | AP Cost | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Move 1 space | 1 AP | Move to adjacent space |
| Fire weapon | Varies | Roll dice, check hit/damage |
| Aim | 1 AP | Bonus to next shot |
| Reload | 1 AP | Reload expended weapon |
| Close door | 1 AP | Seal a doorway |
| Pick up item | 1 AP | Grab equipment |

### Combat

- **Ranged:** Marines roll dice based on weapon type. Range and cover modify hit chances.
- **Melee:** Aliens attack Marines in adjacent spaces. Marines can attempt to defend.
- **Alien acid blood:** Destroyed aliens at close range may cause acid damage.

### Alien Behavior

Aliens follow programmed behavior: move toward nearest Marine, attack in melee. Aliens are fast and deadly in close quarters.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Victory depends on the scenario. Typical objectives include:
- Survive a set number of turns
- Evacuate Marines to a specific location
- Destroy the alien queen
- Complete mission objectives

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Different Marine characters have unique stats (action points, aim skill, morale).
- Weapons have limited ammunition; pulse rifles, flamers, and smartguns behave differently.
- Morale: Marines may panic if isolated or if casualties mount.
- Alien spawning varies by scenario and can increase in intensity.
- Some scenarios include the alien queen with enhanced abilities.

## Player Reference

**Turn Flow:** Marine Phase (spend AP) → Alien Phase (move + attack) → Event Phase (spawn + events)

**Weapon Types:** Pulse Rifle, Smartgun, Flamer, Shotgun -- each with different range, damage, and ammo.

**Key Rule:** Aliens are deadly in melee; keep them at range.
