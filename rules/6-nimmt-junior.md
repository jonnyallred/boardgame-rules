## Overview

6 nimmt! Junior is a family-friendly card game where players travel the world photographing different types of cows. On each turn, a cow card is played into one of four rows. When a row contains all six different cow types, the player who completed it collects those cards. The player with the most cards in their "photo album" wins.

## Components

- 52 cow cards featuring 6 different cows
- 4 row cards
- 6 cow types: Africa (Zebu), Germany (Holstein-Friesian), India (Water Buffalo), Japan (Wagyu), North America (Bison), Scotland (Galloway)

## Setup

1. Place the 4 row cards in the middle of the table in a column. These start each row.
2. Shuffle the cow cards and place them face-down as a deck next to the row cards.

## Turn Structure

Starting with the player who last took a picture of a cow, take turns clockwise:

1. **Reveal** the top card of the deck.
2. **Play** that card into a row of your choice, following the placement rules.
3. **Collect** the row if it is now complete (has all 6 different cow types).

## Actions

| Action | Rule |
|--------|------|
| Play a card | Must add at least one new cow type to the chosen row (a cow not already in that row) |
| No valid row | If all cows on your card already exist in all four rows, play the card in any row |
| Complete a row | If your card gives a row all 6 different cow types, take all cow cards from that row and add them to your photo album face-down |

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- The game ends when the last card of the deck is revealed and played.
- The player with the most cow cards in their photo album wins.
- If tied, all tied players share the victory.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

### Advanced Variant (Ages 6+)
- Deal 3 cow cards to each player before creating the deck.
- Instead of revealing from the deck, play one of your 3 hand cards into a row (same placement rules).
- After playing, draw a new card from the deck to refill to 3 cards. Skip this step when the deck is empty.
- Game ends when the deck is used up and all hand cards are played.
- **Competitive twist:** Optionally, the player with the fewest cards wins instead (closer to the classic 6 nimmt!).

### Pro Variant (Ages 7+)
- Place 4 row cards in a column. Place 1 random cow card face-up next to each row card.
- Deal 3 cow cards to each player; remainder becomes the face-down deck.
- On your turn, play 1 hand card into a row where the rightmost card shares at least one cow type with your card.
- Draw 1 card from the deck after playing (skip when deck is empty).
- **No matching card:** If you cannot play legally, pick a row, take all its cards face-down, then play your card in the empty row.
- **Sixth card in a row:** Take all cards from that row except the one you just played. Leave your card in the now-empty row.
- Game ends when deck is used up and all hands are played. **Fewest cards wins.** Tied players share victory.
