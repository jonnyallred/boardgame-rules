## Overview

Pole Position is a Formula 1 racing board game by Parker Brothers where players race cars around an oval track, using dice and strategic card play. Players navigate a track with straightaways and turns, managing speed and positioning to be the first to complete the required number of laps.

## Components

- 1 race track game board
- 4 racing car pawns
- Dice
- Cards (for speed/movement)

## Setup

1. Place the game board on the table.
2. Each player chooses a car and places it at the starting line.
3. Determine starting order.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. Roll the dice to determine movement.
2. Move your car the corresponding number of spaces along the track.
3. Navigate turns and straightaways according to speed rules.

## Actions

- **Move**: Advance your car along the track based on dice results.
- **Turn management**: Adjust speed when entering turns to avoid penalties.
- **Passing**: Move past opponents on the track following passing rules.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to complete the required number of laps around the track wins the race.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Speed must be managed on turns; excessive speed may result in spinning out.
- Passing rules govern how cars can overtake each other.
- Track position matters for turns and straightaways.
