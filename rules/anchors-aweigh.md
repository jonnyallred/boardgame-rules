## Overview

Anchors Aweigh! (originally Alles an Bord?! in German) is a real-time board game published by Abacusspiele where players quickly decide what supplies they need for a sea journey and race to load their ships. The game combines speed, memory, and tactical decisions as players simultaneously grab equipment tiles from a shared pool. Each ship has room for twelve items, with four hidden under the deck.

## Components

- Player ship boards (one per player, with 12 equipment slots and 4 hidden deck slots)
- Equipment tiles (various types, placed in center of table)
- Colored goods tokens
- Journey cards (determining what equipment is needed)
- Rules booklet

## Setup

1. Give each player a ship board with room for 12 items (4 items hidden under the deck).
2. Place all equipment tiles face-up in the center of the table within reach of all players.
3. Prepare journey cards.
4. Place colored goods tokens within reach.

## Turn Structure

The game lasts 2 rounds, each with 2 phases:

### Phase 1: Loading (Real-time)
- All players simultaneously race to fill their ships with equipment tiles.
- Players may only use one hand to grab tiles.
- Two colored goods must be placed in special slots on the ship.

### Phase 2: Scoring
- Reveal journey requirements and score based on the equipment loaded.

## Actions

### Loading (Real-time Phase)

- Grab equipment tiles from the center pile using only one hand.
- Place tiles on your ship board to fill the 12 equipment slots.
- Strategically select tiles that match the upcoming journey's requirements.
- You may also swap or return tiles during the loading phase.

### Colored Goods

- Each ship has special slots for two colored goods.
- The correct colored goods are needed for scoring bonuses.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

After both rounds, players compare their loaded equipment to the journey requirements. Points are awarded for:
- Matching required equipment types
- Having correct colored goods
- Bonus points for complete sets
- Penalties for missing essential equipment

The player with the most points after 2 rounds wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **One hand only:** Players may only use one hand during the real-time loading phase.
- **Hidden deck slots:** 4 of the 12 equipment slots are hidden under the deck and not visible to opponents.
- **Real-time chaos:** The simultaneous grabbing creates frantic, competitive gameplay.
- **2-round structure:** The game consists of exactly 2 rounds with 2 phases each.
