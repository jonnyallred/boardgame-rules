# Raiders of the North Sea

## Overview

A worker-placement game set during the Viking Age. Players assemble crews, gather provisions, and raid settlements to earn Victory Points. The core mechanic is simple: each turn, place one worker to take an action, then pick up a different worker for a second action. You always start and end your turn with exactly one worker. The game ends when one Fortress remains, all Valkyrie are removed, or all Offerings are made.

## Setup

1. Place board centrally. Shuffle Townsfolk cards into a draw pile.
2. Shuffle Offering tiles; reveal top 3 beside the Long House.
3. Place Valkyrie and all Plunder (Gold, Iron, Livestock) in the bag; randomly draw and place required amounts on each raiding space.
4. Place remaining Valkyrie and Plunder as Main Supply.
5. Players choose Longship boards and matching markers.
6. Starting player gets a black worker; other players get their starting worker per turn order. Deal Townsfolk cards per setup rules.

## Turn Structure

Every turn follows two steps:
1. **Place** your worker on an empty worker space and take that action.
2. **Pick up** a different worker from another space and take that action.

You always begin and end your turn with exactly 1 worker.

## Actions

### Town Actions (place workers in the village)
- **Gate House:** Draw Townsfolk cards
- **Long House:** Hire crew (pay Silver cost, add to your crew area)
- **Armoury:** Collect Provisions
- **Treasury:** Collect Silver
- **Barracks:** Collect Provisions + Silver

### Raiding Actions (place workers at settlements)
Settlements have increasing difficulty: Harbours, Outposts, Monasteries, Fortresses.

To raid: place a worker on the raiding space, meet requirements (crew members, provisions, specific worker colors), then collect the Plunder and Valkyrie shown. Roll dice for bonus VP at Monasteries and Fortresses.

### Offerings
Spend collected Plunder and/or Silver at the Long House to make Offerings for VP.

### Crew Abilities
Hired crew members have special abilities that trigger when hired or provide ongoing effects during raids.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

VP are earned from:
- Raiding settlements
- Collected Plunder
- Making Offerings to the Chieftain
- Valkyrie collected
- Coins earned
- Hired crew with VP values
- Harbour tile bonuses (awarded to top 3 players meeting conditions)

The game ends immediately when one of these occurs:
- Only 1 Fortress raiding space remains
- All Valkyrie tokens have been removed from the supply
- All Offering tiles have been claimed

Final scoring. The player with the most VP wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Worker colors matter: some spaces require specific colors (Black, Grey, or White).
- Black workers are rarer and more powerful; Grey workers are mid-tier.
- When raiding, spent crew members go to a discard area, not back to supply.
- Provisions are consumed during raids.
- Valkyrie are collected during raids and score VP at game end.
