## Overview

Historical Mystery: Bets are Made is a cooperative deduction card game in the Historical Mystery series by Hobby World. Players investigate a mystery set in a historical period, using a deck of 56 cards to interrogate suspects, examine evidence, and piece together clues. Players have exactly 24 moves to solve the case, after which they answer questions about the mystery to determine their success. The game guides players through a gripping narrative with unexpected twists while leaving all major decisions to the players.

## Components

- 56 Cards (investigation cards including suspects, evidence, locations, and events)
- 1 Rulebook
- Case questions sheet with answers

## Setup

1. Sort cards as directed by the rulebook.
2. Lay out the initial case introduction cards.
3. Set the move counter to 24.
4. Each player reviews the case premise.

## Turn Structure

Players take turns performing investigation actions:
1. **Choose an action** — Select which card or lead to investigate.
2. **Resolve** — Read the corresponding card(s) and gather information.
3. **Reduce move counter** — Each investigation action costs 1 move.
4. **Discuss** — Share findings and theories with other players.

## Actions

### Interrogate Suspects
- Question a suspect by reading their card.
- Reveals testimony, alibis, or suspicious details.
- Costs 1 move.

### Examine Evidence
- Inspect physical evidence or documents.
- May confirm or contradict suspect testimony.
- Costs 1 move.

### Visit Locations
- Explore a scene related to the crime.
- May reveal new suspects, evidence, or narrative events.
- Costs 1 move.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

After using all 24 moves (or choosing to stop early):
1. Players answer a series of case questions.
2. Questions cover the culprit, motive, method, and key plot details.
3. More correct answers = better score.
4. A perfect score represents a completely solved case.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Cooperative:** All players work together and share information openly.
- **24-move limit:** Careful budgeting is essential; not all cards can be investigated in a single game.
- **Single play-through:** Once solved, the mystery cannot be replayed with the same group (as answers are known).
- **Solo compatible:** Fully playable by one player.
- **Historical setting:** The mystery takes place in a specific historical period with period-appropriate characters.
- **Series game:** Uses the same core system as other Historical Mystery titles (Death at the Ball, Disappearance in the Sky, The Witch, Treasures of the Galleon).
