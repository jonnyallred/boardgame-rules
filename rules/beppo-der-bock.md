## Overview

Beppo der Bock (Beppo the Billy Goat) is a children's dexterity and racing game for 2-4 players aged 5 and up, published in 2007 by HUCH! & friends in collaboration with Oberschwabische Magnetspiele. It won the Kinderspiel des Jahres (Children's Game of the Year) award in 2007. Players race their wooden figurines along a track by launching a metal ball at a magnetic goat named Beppo. Where Beppo lands after being struck determines how far the active player moves, but Beppo can also kick other players' pieces back to the start.

## Components

- Game board with a circular track and central area
- 1 magnetic goat figure (Beppo)
- 4 colored wooden player pawns
- 1 metal ball
- Magnetic mechanism embedded in the board

## Setup

Place the game board on a flat surface. Each player chooses a colored pawn and places it on the starting space. Place Beppo the goat on the designated magnetic spot in the center of the board. Place the metal ball nearby.

## Turn Structure

Players take turns in clockwise order. On each turn:

1. **Launch the Ball**: The active player places the metal ball and flicks or rolls it toward Beppo.
2. **Beppo Reacts**: The magnetic goat figure is knocked away by the ball's impact.
3. **Check Landing**: Determine which space on the board Beppo has landed on or nearest to.
4. **Move Pawn**: The active player moves their pawn forward a number of spaces determined by where Beppo landed.
5. **Check for Kicks**: If Beppo landed on or passed over any player pawns, those pawns are sent back to the starting space.

## Actions

- **Flicking the Ball**: The active player launches the metal ball toward Beppo. The skill lies in controlling the force and direction to land Beppo on a favorable space without knocking opponents back to start.
- **Movement**: Move your pawn forward the number of spaces indicated by Beppo's landing position. The goal is to reach the finish space first.
- **Beppo's Kick**: Any pawn that Beppo contacts or passes over during his movement is "kicked" back to start. This applies to all players' pawns, including the active player's own pawn.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to move their pawn from the starting space to the last space on the track wins the game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Beppo Off the Board**: If the ball or Beppo goes off the board, the turn is wasted and the next player goes.
- **Own Pawn Kick**: Beppo can kick your own pawn back to start if he lands on it, so players must be careful with their aim.
- **Magnetic Attraction**: The embedded magnets cause Beppo to react unpredictably to the ball's impact, adding an element of chaos.
- **Multiple Kicks**: Beppo can kick multiple pawns in a single turn if his trajectory passes over or lands on multiple pieces.
- **Young Players**: The game's simple rules and physical component make it accessible to children as young as 5.
