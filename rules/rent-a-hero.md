# Rent a Hero

## Overview

Rent a Hero is a simple bluffing and strategy card game for 3-5 players, published by IELLO. Players compete to recruit a band of 6 unique heroes from a deck of 77 cards (11 sets of heroes numbered 1-7). Each turn, the active player draws a card and passes it face-down to the next player, who must decide whether to recruit the hero or pass it along. The catch: if you recruit a hero you already have, BOTH copies are discarded. The first player to assemble 6 different heroes wins.

## Components

- 77 Cards (11 copies each of heroes numbered 1-7)
- 5 Player aid cards
- Rulebook

## Setup

1. Shuffle the entire deck.
2. Deal 2 face-up hero cards to each player to form the start of their Band of Heroes. If a player receives two of the same hero, discard one and draw replacements until they have 2 different heroes.
3. Deal 5 face-down cards to each player as their hand.
4. Place remaining cards face-down as the common draw deck.
5. Randomly select the starting player.

## Turn Structure

On the active player's turn:

1. **Draw and Reveal:** Draw the top card from the deck and place it face-up on the discard pile. Note the required strength number shown.
2. **Select and Pass:** Choose a card from your hand matching the revealed strength and pass it face-down to the player on your left.
3. **Receive Decision:** The receiving player decides:
   - **Recruit:** Reveal the card and add it to their Band of Heroes, OR
   - **Pass Along:** Pass the card face-down to the next player, who faces the same choice.
4. **Card Returns to Sender:** If the card passes all the way around without being recruited, the active player MUST recruit it.

## Actions

- **Recruit a Hero:** Add a received hero card face-up to your Band of Heroes.
- **Pass Along:** Decline a card and pass it face-down to the next player.
- **Request a Clue:** Give one of your hand cards to the active player and ask a yes/no question about the passed card.
- **Use Hero Power:** Each hero number has a unique power that can be used once per game (rotate the card sideways to mark it as used).
- **Eliminate Duplicates:** If you recruit a hero you already have in your band, BOTH copies are immediately discarded.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- **Immediate Win:** The first player to have 6 different heroes in their Band of Heroes wins.
- **Deck Exhaustion:** If the deck runs out, the player with the most heroes in their band wins. Ties broken by: fewest eliminated heroes, then fewest hero powers used.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Duplicate Penalty:** Recruiting a hero you already own causes BOTH copies to be discarded, setting you back significantly. This is the core bluffing tension.
- **No Matching Card:** If you have no card matching the revealed strength, draw from the deck and discard a hand card.
- **Hero Powers:** Each hero number (1-7) has a unique ability usable once per game. Powers are marked by rotating the card horizontally.
- **Clue Mechanism:** Players can trade hand cards for yes/no information about the passed card, adding a deduction element.
- **Passing Strategy:** Since a card that returns to the sender must be recruited, the active player should avoid passing heroes they already have.
