## Overview

Bau Cua Ca Cop (Gourd-Crab-Shrimp-Fish) is a traditional Vietnamese dice betting game. Instead of numbered dots, the three dice feature six images: a gourd (bau), a crab (cua), a shrimp (ca/tom), a fish (ca), a rooster (ga), and a tiger/stag (cop). Players place wagers on the images they predict will appear on the dice. It is traditionally played during Vietnamese Lunar New Year (Tet) celebrations.

## Components

- 3 custom dice (each face shows one of 6 images: gourd, crab, shrimp, fish, rooster, tiger)
- 1 game board/mat with 6 image sections
- Betting tokens (pennies, beans, chips, or any uniform counters)

## Setup

1. Place the game board in the center of the playing area.
2. Distribute an equal number of betting tokens to each player.
3. Set aside a supply of extra tokens for payouts.
4. Agree on the number of rounds to play before starting.

## Turn Structure

Each round follows these steps:

1. **Place bets:** Each player places one or more betting tokens on the board image(s) they think will appear on the dice. Players may bet on multiple images.
2. **Roll dice:** One designated player (the dealer) rolls all 3 dice.
3. **Call results:** The dealer announces which images appear on the dice.
4. **Resolve payouts:** Compare dice results to bets and pay out accordingly.

## Actions

- **Bet:** Place any number of your tokens on one or more of the 6 images on the board.
- **Roll:** The dealer rolls 3 dice simultaneously.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- For each die that matches an image you bet on, you receive tokens equal to your bet on that image:
  - **1 die matches:** Win 1x your bet (you get your bet back plus an equal amount)
  - **2 dice match:** Win 2x your bet
  - **3 dice match:** Win 3x your bet
- If none of the dice match your bet, you lose your wagered tokens.
- After the agreed number of rounds, the player with the most tokens wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Players may bet on as many different images as they wish in a single round.
- If a player runs out of tokens, they are eliminated (or may borrow from the supply depending on house rules).
- The dealer role may rotate each round or remain fixed.
- The game can be played purely for fun with no gambling by using beans, chips, or other non-monetary tokens.
