## Overview

Garden Party is a family board game published by Playroom Entertainment in 2006. Players compete to create the most beautiful garden at a gardening contest. Each player moves around a colorful board, collecting plant tokens and cards to fill their garden with 14 different types of plants at various life stages. Players can use magical tools to help their garden grow, but must also deal with hazards like drought and pests, as well as sabotage from opponents who can trick, steal, or trap them.

## Components

- 1 game board (colorful garden paths with plant spaces and special spaces)
- 1 customized die
- 6 game figurines (player pawns)
- 24 water tokens
- 111 plant tokens (representing different plants at various growth stages)
- 128 cards (plant cards, event cards, tool cards)
- 1 rules booklet

## Setup

1. Place the game board in the center of the table.
2. Each player chooses a figurine and places it on the starting space.
3. Distribute plant tokens on designated board spaces.
4. Shuffle the card deck and deal starting hands.
5. Place water tokens in a central supply.
6. Youngest player goes first.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:

### 1. Roll the Die
Roll the customized die to determine movement or a special action.

### 2. Move Your Figurine
Move your figurine along the garden path the indicated number of spaces.

### 3. Take an Action
Based on the space you land on:
- **Plant space:** Collect the matching plant card or token for your garden.
- **Water space:** Collect water tokens needed for growing plants.
- **Tool space:** Draw a magical tool card to help your garden.
- **Event space:** Draw an event card (may be positive or negative).
- **Garden Party space:** Receive a small bonus (refreshment/rest).

### 4. Play Cards (Optional)
Play cards from your hand to grow plants, protect your garden, or interfere with opponents.

### 5. Pass
Play passes to the next player clockwise.

## Actions

### Planting
Collect plant tokens and cards to add plants to your garden. Plants come in different types and growth stages.

### Watering
Water tokens are needed to advance plants through their growth stages. Collect water from water spaces and use it to nurture your garden.

### Magical Tools
Tool cards provide special abilities:
- Accelerate plant growth
- Protect against hazards
- Duplicate plants
- Other beneficial effects

### Sabotage
Cards can be used offensively:
- Steal plants from opponents
- Set traps in other gardens
- Cause drought or pest attacks on rival gardens

### Hazard Defense
Protect your garden against cruel turns of fortune:
- **Drought:** Withers plants unless you have water tokens or antidote cards.
- **Pests:** Attack and destroy plants unless countered with bug killer or similar cards.
- **Flooding:** Damages gardens unless protected by insurance cards.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

**Win:** The first player to plant all 16 spaces of their garden with 4 different types of plant cards wins the game.

Players must have a complete, diverse garden -- simply having 16 plants of one type is not sufficient.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

### Plant Diversity
Your garden must contain at least 4 different types of plants among its 16 spaces. Strategic collection of varied plants is essential.

### Water Management
Water tokens are a limited resource. Running out of water at critical moments can stall your garden's progress while opponents advance.

### Garden Party Spaces
Landing on a Garden Party space provides a rest bonus. The exact benefit varies by game variant.

### Event Cards
Events can dramatically change the game state. Some events affect all players, while others target only the active player.

### Stolen Plants
If an opponent steals a plant from your garden, you must refill that space with a new plant to complete your garden.
