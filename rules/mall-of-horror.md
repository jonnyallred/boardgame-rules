## Overview

Mall of Horror is a survival board game where players control groups of characters trapped in a zombie-infested shopping mall. Each round, zombies arrive and attack locations. Players negotiate, vote, and sacrifice characters to survive. The player with the most victory points from surviving characters wins.

## Components

- 1 game board (mall with locations)
- 5 closed markers
- 21 character tokens (3 per player: Pin-Up, Tough Guy, Gunman)
- 30 zombie figures
- 6 voting wheels
- 4 dice
- 1 dice box
- 21 action cards
- 1 Chief of Security badge
- Rulebook

## Setup

1. Each player selects a color and receives 3 character tokens (Pin-Up, Tough Guy, Gunman) and a voting wheel.
2. The oldest player becomes Chief of Security.
3. In 3-4 player games, Location 2 is closed.
4. Each player rolls 2 dice to place characters in matching locations (continue until all placed).
5. The Chief rolls 4 dice to place initial zombies.

## Turn Structure

Each round has these phases:

### A. Search the Truck
Characters in the Parking Lot vote on who searches the truck. The winner examines 3 action cards, keeps 1, gives 1 to another player, and puts 1 at the bottom of the deck.

### B. Elect Chief of Security
Characters in Security HQ vote for a new Chief. If no one is elected, the current Chief remains.

### C. Zombie Arrival
The Chief rolls 4 dice under cover of the dice box. Players with Security Camera cards may peek. Zombies are placed at locations matching dice results.

### D. Choose Destinations
Players secretly select locations for their characters using voting wheels. No location may exceed its capacity.

### E. Move Characters
Characters move simultaneously. If a location becomes condemned (8+ zombies), it closes permanently. Overflow characters go to the Parking Lot.

### F. Attack of the Zombies
Zombies attack locations with characters. When zombies equal or exceed character count at a location, players vote to sacrifice one character (the victim is eliminated). Tough Guys count as 2 defenders.

## Actions

### Action Cards
- **Hardware:** Reduces zombie threat by 1
- **Weapon:** Kills 1-2 zombies
- **Hidden:** Protects a character from being voted as victim; that character cannot vote but counts toward defense
- **Security Camera:** View zombie arrival dice
- **Sprint:** Change destination during movement phase
- **Threat:** Discard before voting for extra votes

### Voting
- Players vote simultaneously using wheels.
- Each character in a location gets 1 vote.
- Gunmen get an extra vote when present.
- Tied votes trigger re-voting.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The game ends when all surviving characters occupy the same non-Parking Lot location, or only 4 characters remain total.

- Each character token has a victory point value.
- The player with the most victory points from surviving characters wins.
- Ties broken by unused action cards.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Parking Lot:** Every zombie in the Parking Lot feeds automatically. All zombies leave after feeding.
- **Supermarket:** Only 4 zombies needed to enter, regardless of character count.
- **Eliminated players:** Those who lose all characters return as zombies in subsequent rounds, placing a zombie at their chosen location during the destination phase.
- **Condemned locations:** Permanently closed; characters and zombies inside are lost.
