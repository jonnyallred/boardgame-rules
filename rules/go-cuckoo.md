## Overview

Go Cuckoo! is a dexterity game where players build a nest from colored sticks inside a tin can, then try to balance eggs on the nest structure. Players draw sticks and attempt to find matching-color-ended sticks, placing them across the top of the can to create a nest platform. The first player to place all their eggs and successfully perch Kiki the Cuckoo on top of the nest wins.

## Components

- 1 tin can (game container and nest base)
- 70 colored wooden sticks (both ends colored)
- 20 cuckoo eggs (divided among players)
- 1 Kiki Cuckoo figurine

## Setup

1. Divide the eggs equally among all players.
2. Place all 70 sticks loosely inside the tin can, creating a tangled base structure.
3. Place Kiki Cuckoo to the side -- she will be needed at the end.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:

1. **Draw sticks:** Draw up to 3 sticks from the can, one at a time.
2. **Check for matching ends:** Look for a stick where both ends are the same color.
3. **Place sticks:** Lay drawn sticks horizontally across the top of the can to build the nest.
4. **Place an egg:** If you drew a matching-color stick, place one of your eggs on the nest.
5. If you did not find a matching stick within 3 draws, simply place the drawn sticks on the nest without adding an egg.

## Actions

### Drawing Sticks

- Draw sticks one at a time from the can.
- You may stop drawing after finding a matching stick (same color on both ends).
- Maximum of 3 stick draws per turn.

### Placing Sticks

- Lay sticks horizontally across the opening of the can to form a lattice-like nest structure.
- Sticks should rest on the rim of the can and on other sticks.

### Placing Eggs

- Only permitted if you drew a stick with matching colors on both ends.
- Place one egg from your supply onto the nest structure.
- The egg must balance on the sticks.

### Penalties for Knocking Things Over

- **Knock over a stick:** Replace it; your turn ends immediately.
- **Knock eggs inside the can:** Take penalty eggs from the player who currently has the most eggs remaining.
- **Knock eggs outside the can:** You must take those fallen eggs as a penalty, adding them to your supply.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- When you have placed all your eggs, you must place Kiki Cuckoo on top of the nest on your next turn.
- The **first player to successfully place all eggs and perch Kiki Cuckoo on the nest wins**.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Egg penalty source:** When eggs fall inside the can, the penalty eggs come from the player with the most eggs remaining, not from the can.
- **Kiki Cuckoo placement:** You cannot attempt to place Kiki until all your eggs are on the nest. If Kiki falls off during placement, your turn ends and you must try again next turn.
- **Stick matching:** Only sticks where BOTH ends are the same color allow you to place an egg. Two-color sticks do not qualify.
- **Dexterity challenge:** The game becomes progressively harder as more sticks and eggs are placed, making the structure more precarious.
- **Equal egg distribution:** If eggs don't divide evenly, remaining eggs are set aside.

## Player Reference

**Turn sequence:** Draw up to 3 sticks > Place sticks on nest > Place egg (if matching stick found)

**Matching stick:** Both ends same color = place an egg

**Penalties:**
- Stick knocked over: replace it, turn ends
- Eggs fall inside can: take eggs from player with most remaining
- Eggs fall outside can: take those eggs

**Win condition:** Place all eggs + place Kiki Cuckoo on nest
