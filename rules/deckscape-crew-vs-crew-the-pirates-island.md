## Overview

Deckscape Crew vs Crew: The Pirates' Island is a competitive card-based escape room game for 2-8 players, split into two teams. Unlike other Deckscape games which are purely cooperative, this entry pits two crews of pirates against each other. Both teams race to solve puzzles and find the treasure first. The game uses the core Deckscape system of numbered cards with puzzles on one side and answers on the back, but adds team-based competition.

## Components

- 1 Deck of cards (divided into shared and team-specific sections)
- Players provide: paper, pencil, and a clock/timer

## Setup

1. Divide players into two teams.
2. Remove the deck from the box without shuffling.
3. Follow the initial card instructions to divide certain cards between teams.
4. Prepare paper, pencil, and a timer.
5. Start the timer and begin.

## Turn Structure

Both teams work simultaneously on puzzles. Some puzzles are shared; others are team-specific. Teams race to solve puzzles faster than their opponents.

## Actions

### Solving Puzzles
- Read each puzzle card and work with your team to find the answer.
- Flip the card to check. Errors are tracked per team.

### Team Competition
- Both teams solve puzzles in parallel.
- The team that completes their puzzles first or with fewer errors wins.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The team with the better performance (fewer errors, faster time) wins the race to the treasure.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Designed for a single play-through.
- The competitive element is unique to the Crew vs Crew variant.
- Both teams should be roughly equal in size for fair play.
- Uses the core Deckscape card system.
