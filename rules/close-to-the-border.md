## Overview

Close to the Border is a board game where players navigate toward a border or boundary, competing to be the first to reach the goal or accumulate the most points along the way. The game involves movement, strategy, and interaction between players as they race across the board.

## Components

- Game board with a path toward the border
- Player tokens/pawns
- Movement cards or dice
- Event/action cards
- Score tracking mechanism

## Setup

1. Place the board in the center.
2. Each player selects a token and places it at the starting position.
3. Shuffle event/action cards if applicable.
4. Determine a starting player.

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Determine movement** — roll dice or play a movement card.
2. **Move your token** along the path.
3. **Resolve the space** — follow any instructions on the space you land on.
4. **Draw or play action cards** if applicable.

## Actions

### Move
- Advance your token the number of spaces indicated by your dice roll or card.
- Some spaces may have special effects (advance, retreat, skip turn, draw card).

### Use Action Cards
- Play cards from your hand to affect your movement, hinder opponents, or gain advantages.
- Cards may allow extra movement, blocking opponents, or other special effects.

### Interact
- Landing on or passing opponent tokens may trigger interactions.
- Some spaces create opportunities for player interaction.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- The first player to reach the border (finish line) wins, or
- Players score points along the way and the player with the most points at the end wins.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Specific space effects may vary by edition.
- The theme of "border crossing" provides the narrative framework.
- The game supports 2-4 players with scaling rules.
- Quick play time makes it suitable for casual gaming.
