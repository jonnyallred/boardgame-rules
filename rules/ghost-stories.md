## Overview

Ghost Stories is a cooperative game where 1-4 players are Taoist priests (Fat-Si) defending a village from waves of ghosts and ultimately the incarnation of Wu-Feng, Lord of the Nine Hells. Players take turns moving through a 3x3 village grid, using dice and Tao tokens to exorcise ghosts that accumulate on their personal boards. The game features 4 difficulty levels (Initiation, Normal, Nightmare, Hell) and is notoriously challenging.

## Components

- 4 game boards (double-sided, one per Taoist color)
- 9 Village tiles (double-sided: active/haunted)
- 3 Tao dice + 1 special Tao die (grey) + 1 Curse die
- 55 Ghost cards (blue backs) + 10 Wu-Feng incarnation cards (red backs)
- 20 Tao tokens (5 colors: yellow/sticky rice, green/silver bells, red/incense, blue/mirrors, black/coins)
- 20 Qi tokens (life points)
- 4 Taoist figurines
- 4 Yin-Yang tokens
- 8 Haunting figurines + 2 Buddha figurines
- 1 Inactive Tao marker, 4 Inactive Power tokens, 3 Neutral Power tokens
- 1 Enfeeblement Mantra token
- 2 play aids, 1 score sheet

## Setup

1. Randomly arrange 9 Village tiles in a 3x3 grid (all active/face-up).
2. Place 4 game boards along the edges, each facing a player. Randomly assign colors and board sides.
3. Each player takes: 4 Qi tokens, their Yin-Yang token, 1 Tao token of their color, 1 black Tao token. Place Taoist figurine on the central village tile.
4. Place 2 Buddha figurines on the Buddhist Temple tile.
5. Prepare Ghost deck: take 1 random Wu-Feng incarnation, shuffle 55 ghost cards, place 10 ghost cards on bottom, then the Wu-Feng card, then the remaining 45 ghost cards on top. (Wu-Feng appears near the end.)

## Turn Structure

Each turn has 2 phases:

### Yin Phase (Ghosts)

**Step 1 — Ghosts' Actions:** Apply all central-stone abilities of ghosts on your board:
- **Haunters:** Advance their Haunting figurine one position (card -> board icon -> haunts a village tile). A tile is haunted every 2 turns per Haunter.
- **Tormentors:** Roll the Curse die and apply the result.

**Step 2 — Board Overrun Check:** If all 3 ghost spaces on your board are occupied, lose 1 Qi. Skip Step 3.

**Step 3 — Ghost Arrival:** Draw the top ghost card and place it:
- Colored ghosts (R/G/B/Y) go on the matching color board. If full, active player chooses any board.
- Black ghosts go on the active player's board. If full, choose any board.
- Apply left-stone abilities immediately upon placement.
- If all 12 spaces across all boards are full, lose 1 Qi instead of placing.

### Yang Phase (Taoist)

**1) Move (optional):** Move to any adjacent tile (including diagonal). Not mandatory.

**2) Request help from a villager OR Attempt an exorcism** (choose one):

**2a) Villager Help:** Use the ability of the Village tile you occupy (see play aid).

**2b) Exorcism:** Target a ghost on an adjacent space (from edge tiles only — no exorcism from center tile, two possible targets from corner tiles). Roll 3 Tao dice. To succeed, get a number of the ghost's color (or white) >= the ghost's resistance. May spend Tao tokens of matching color to supplement dice. If successful, ghost is discarded. Apply right-stone abilities (curses before rewards).

**3) Place a Buddha (optional):** If you have a Buddha from the Buddhist Temple, place it on an empty ghost space adjacent to your tile. Any ghost later placed there is immediately discarded (no abilities trigger).

**Yin-Yang power** may be used before or after any Yang phase step (not mid-action): either request help from any villager (regardless of position) or un-haunt a village tile.

## Actions

### Exorcism Details
- Roll 3 Tao dice (white = any color). Need hits >= ghost resistance in the ghost's color.
- May spend Tao tokens (matching color) to supplement.
- If on a corner tile, may target 2 adjacent ghosts with a single roll.
- Other Taoists on the same tile may contribute their Tao tokens.
- If dice are sufficient to exorcise, you must do so (even if undesired).

### Haunting a Village Tile
When a haunting figurine reaches the board edge, flip the first active village tile in front of that ghost face-down (haunted). If already haunted, haunt the next tile in line.

### Sharing Tao Tokens
Only during exorcism, and only if Taoists share the same tile. No other giving/exchanging allowed.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

### Victory
Exorcise the incarnation of Wu-Feng (in Nightmare/Hell: all incarnations).

### Defeat (any one triggers loss)
1. All Taoists are dead (0 Qi each)
2. A 3rd village tile becomes haunted
3. The last ghost card is drawn with Wu-Feng still alive

### Score Calculation
- +10 points for victory (Initiation/Normal/Nightmare), +20 for Hell
- +1 per remaining Qi
- +1 per ghost card still in deck (if won), -1 per card (if lost)
- -3 per dead Taoist
- -4 per haunted village tile
- +2/+4/+6 for exorcising 1st/2nd/3rd incarnation (Nightmare/Hell)

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

### Taoist Powers (one per player, determined by board side)

| Color | Power 1 | Power 2 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Yellow | **Enfeeblement Mantra:** Reduce any ghost's resistance by 1 | **Bottomless Pockets:** Take 1 free Tao token before moving |
| Red | **Dance of Twin Winds:** Move another Taoist before your move | **Dance of Peaks:** Fly to any village tile (not just adjacent) |
| Blue | **Second Wind:** Use villager help twice OR attempt 2 exorcisms | **Heavenly Gust:** Use villager help AND attempt exorcism in same turn |
| Green | **Strength of Mountain:** Roll 4th Tao die, never roll Curse die | **Gods' Favourite:** Re-roll any Tao/Curse dice (keep second result) |

### Death of a Taoist
- At 0 Qi, the Taoist dies. Lose all belongings. Figurine placed lying on Cemetery tile.
- Dead player still takes turns (Yin phase only) and may advise others.
- Can be revived via Cemetery tile action by other Taoists.
- Dead Taoist's board becomes a neutral board.

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Qi Start | Starting Tokens | Incarnations | Special |
|-------|----------|----------------|--------------|---------|
| Initiation | 4 | Color + Black Tao | 1 | — |
| Normal | 3 | Color Tao only | 1 | — |
| Nightmare | 3 | Color Tao only | 3-4 | Multiple incarnations spread in deck |
| Hell | 3 | Color Tao only | 3-4 | No starting Yin-Yang |

### Player Count Adjustments
- **3 players:** Remove 5 random ghost cards before adding incarnation(s).
- **2 players:** Remove 10 random ghost cards. Players must sit facing each other.
- **1 player:** Remove 15 ghost cards. Take 1 each of R/G/B/Y Tao tokens. Use 3 neutral power tokens. Red board must show Dance of Peaks power.

### Incarnation Abilities (examples)
- **Hope Killer:** Resistance 8 (2 each of blue/red/green/yellow)
- **Nameless:** Resistance 5 (1 each of all colors), white dice sides don't count
- **Vampire Lord:** Simply a Haunter with resistance 4
- **Uncatchable:** Must be on a space with a Buddha to exorcise

## Player Reference

**Turn Sequence:**
1. **Yin Phase:** Ghost actions (Haunters advance, Tormentors roll) -> Board overrun check -> Draw ghost
2. **Yang Phase:** Move (optional) -> Villager help OR Exorcism -> Place Buddha (optional)
3. Yin-Yang may be used before/after any Yang step

**Curse Die Results:**
- No effect
- Haunt first active tile in front of ghost
- Bring a ghost into play
- Discard all your Tao tokens
- Lose 1 Qi

**Key Thresholds:**
- 3 haunted tiles = defeat
- 4 in a row of same color on ghost spaces = n/a (this is not that kind of game)
- 12 total ghost spaces across 4 boards (3 each)
