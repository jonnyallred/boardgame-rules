## Overview

Alpha Nature is an educational trivia and word game where players move through the letters of the alphabet from A to Z, answering nature-themed questions along the way. Players advance one space at a time across the board and must name items from nature beginning with the letter they land on, in a category determined by a die roll. The game includes two difficulty levels, making it suitable for families with children as young as 8.

## Components

- Game board with alphabetical spaces (A through Z) and Alpha Nature card spaces between them
- 8-sided die (for category selection)
- Alpha Nature question cards (two difficulty levels)
- Player pawns (one per player)

## Setup

1. Place the board centrally.
2. Each player chooses a pawn and places it on the Start space.
3. Separate the Alpha Nature cards by difficulty level and choose Level 1 (ages 8+) or Level 2 (ages 12+).
4. Place the chosen deck face-down near the board.

## Turn Structure

On each turn, a player:

1. Rolls the die or advances to the next space.
2. If landing on a **letter space**, rolls the 8-sided die to determine the nature category and names an item.
3. If landing on an **Alpha Nature card space**, draws and answers a question card.
4. Advances to the next letter if successful, or stays in place if unsuccessful.

## Actions

### Letter Spaces

When landing on a letter space, roll the 8-sided die. The die determines which of the following nature categories applies:

| Die Result | Category |
|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Trees |
| 2 | Edible Plants |
| 3 | Space |
| 4 | Animals |
| 5 | Water |
| 6 | Flowers |
| 7 | Land |
| 8 | Any category |

The player must name something from the selected category that starts with the letter on the space they occupy. For example, landing on "E" and rolling "Animals" requires naming an animal starting with E (e.g., "Elephant").

**No repeats:** A player may not name something that has already been used by any player during the game.

### Alpha Nature Card Spaces

Between letter spaces are Alpha Nature card spaces. Landing on one requires the player to draw a card and answer its question correctly to advance to the next letter space. Cards come in two difficulty levels:
- **Level 1:** Ages 8 and up
- **Level 2:** Ages 12 and up

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to move through the entire alphabet from A to Z and reach the finish wins the game.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Previously used answers are tracked and cannot be repeated by any player.
- If a player cannot name a valid item, they do not advance.
- The "Any category" die result allows the player to choose the easiest category for that letter.
- Difficult letters (Q, X, Z) can be challenging and may allow looser interpretations at the group's discretion.
- The two difficulty levels allow mixed-age groups to play together with appropriate challenge.

## Player Reference

**Turn:** Advance to next space → Roll die for category (letter space) or draw card (card space) → Name item / answer question → Advance if correct

**Categories:** Trees, Edible Plants, Space, Animals, Water, Flowers, Land, Any

**Win Condition:** First to reach Z wins.
