## Overview

LEADERS: The Combined Strategy Game is a hybrid board-and-app strategy game where players lead nations through diplomacy, warfare, and economic development. The game combines a physical board game with a companion app that manages hidden information, resolves combat, and tracks game state. Players compete to become the dominant world power by controlling territories, managing resources, and forging or breaking alliances.

## Components

- Game board (world map divided into territories)
- Nation cards
- Resource tokens
- Military unit tokens
- Diplomacy cards
- Event cards
- Dice
- Quick guide rulebook
- Companion app (required for play)

## Setup

1. Launch the LEADERS app on a smart device.
2. Each player selects a nation.
3. Place starting units and resources per the app's setup instructions.
4. The app manages turn order and hidden information.

## Turn Structure

Each round consists of phases managed by the app:
1. **Resource Phase**: Collect resources from controlled territories.
2. **Diplomacy Phase**: Negotiate alliances, trade deals, or declare war.
3. **Action Phase**: Move units, build structures, recruit military forces.
4. **Combat Phase**: Resolve conflicts between opposing forces (managed by the app).
5. **Event Phase**: App-driven events that affect the game state.

## Actions

- **Move units**: Deploy military forces to adjacent territories.
- **Build**: Construct buildings and infrastructure in controlled territories.
- **Recruit**: Train new military units using resources.
- **Diplomacy**: Propose alliances, trade, or war declarations through the app.
- **Special abilities**: Each nation has unique abilities that can be activated.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- The player who achieves the dominant position (most territories, strongest military, most resources) at the end of the game wins.
- Specific victory conditions are managed and announced by the companion app.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- The companion app is **required** to play; it manages hidden information and combat resolution.
- Diplomacy is conducted through the app, allowing secret negotiations.
- Events are randomized by the app and can dramatically shift the game state.
- The game supports both cooperative alliances and backstabbing.
- Multiple game modes available through the app.
