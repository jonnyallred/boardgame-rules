## Overview

ConHex is an abstract strategy game for two players (Yellow and Red). Both aim to complete a contiguous chain of connected cells between the two sides of their color on the board. Draws are not possible. Cells are claimed by surrounding them with pegs.

## Components

- 1 ConHex board with 41 cells and 69 holes
- 26 rectangular markers of each color (Yellow and Red)
- 26 pegs of each color (Yellow and Red)
- Carrying case

## Setup

1. Place the empty ConHex board between the two players.
2. Each player selects a color (Yellow or Red) and takes all 26 pegs and 26 rectangles of their color.
3. Red starts the game.

## Turn Structure

Players alternate turns. On each turn, the current player places one of their pegs on an empty hole on the board. Placing a peg may conquer one or more adjacent cells.

## Actions

**Placing a Peg:**
- Place one peg of your color on any empty hole on the board.

**Conquering a Cell:**
- A cell is conquered by the first player to occupy at least half of its surrounding holes.
- When a cell is conquered, a rectangle of the conquering player's color is placed on that cell.
- The central cell has five surrounding holes and is conquered by the player who occupies any three of those holes. No rectangle needs to be placed for the central cell.

**Swap Option:**
- On Yellow's first move (the second move of the game), Yellow may elect to swap colors instead of placing a peg. This prevents overly strong opening moves by Red.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The first player to complete a contiguous chain of connected cells of their color between the two sides of their color wins the game. Ties are not possible due to the board geometry.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- The swap rule only applies to Yellow's very first turn.
- Once a cell is conquered, it cannot change ownership.
- A single peg placement can conquer multiple cells simultaneously if it completes the required majority around more than one cell.
- The central cell requires 3 out of 5 holes (majority), while standard cells require half of their surrounding holes.

## Player Reference

| Cell Type | Holes Around It | Pegs Needed to Conquer |
|-----------|----------------|----------------------|
| Standard edge cell | 3-4 | 2 (half or more) |
| Standard interior cell | 6 | 3 (half) |
| Central cell | 5 | 3 |

**Key rules:**
- Red goes first
- Yellow may swap colors on first turn
- Place 1 peg per turn
- Conquer cells by occupying at least half of surrounding holes
- Win by connecting your two sides with a chain of conquered cells
