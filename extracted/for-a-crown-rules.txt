At war for as long as they can remember, your families have decided it’s high time 
to form a single kingdom. But who will wear the first crown? Competition will be tough, 
are you ready to do what it takes to gain the throne?
Over four rounds, recruit Mercenaries, send Masked Bandits to your rivals, 
increase your influence, or cast a bit of magic to make your opponents lose Rubies 
and ensure you’re crowned at the end of this battle royale!
