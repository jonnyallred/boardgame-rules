Description
Join other ambitious guild leaders in mining and developing the
famous City of Silver during its period of rapid economic growth
and expansion in the 14th century — from the first discovery of
silver near the cistercian monastery to the construction of Kutná
Hora, which quickly became one of the most important cities in
central Europe.
Kutná Hora is a historical, asymmetrical city building euro game for
2-4 players that features a real-life supply and demand experience
where every action you take has an impact on the game’s dynamic
economic systems.
Mine ore and smelt it into a fortune of silver for expanding this
beautiful historic city, but take care to balance your personal goal
advancement with the need to further the city’s growth. Everything
is connected, and sometimes the path to personal victory relies on
the prosperity of the many.

How to make a complaint?
B2B only
Terms and conditions for resolving any new complaints of damaged
games during transport: iva@czechgames.com
Reach us and provide following documents and pictures:
• We need a scan of a document stating that the shipment
arrived damaged.
• Provide a picture of a whole pallet with the damage.
• Provide a picture of the damaged games (ex. 3 damaged
Codenames = one picture with 3 Codenames where the
damage is obvious).
We will evaluate the damage and issue a credit note for you:
• We do not accept complaints when only the game foil is
damaged a bit.
• If there is only a box damaged and the game itself is still ok,
you receive 50% discount.
• If the game is damaged a lot, you receive 80% discount.
