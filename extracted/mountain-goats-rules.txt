18 Mountain Cards: 4x(5,6), 3x(7,8), 2x(9,10).
4 Bonus Point Tokens: 15p, 12p, 9p, 6p.
57 Point Tokens: 12x5p,11x6p,10x7p,9x8p,8x9p,7x10p.
24 Mountain Goats: 6 for each player
4 Dice
A 20 minute game, for 2 to 4 players
