GAME OVERVIEW 
In this game you’ll be helping your hedgehog reach his house by rolling a fuzzy ball and 
collecting such forest goodies as mushrooms, leaves and apples. Choose the path wisely, use your 
dexterity skills and add just a little bit of luck in order to get home as fast as possible.
In a cooperative game, all players are 
helping one hedgehog — you are running 
away from the fox that is following you! Your 
goal is to lead your hedgehog to the house 
before the fox catches you.
In a competitive game, your hedgehogs 
will race against each other! Your aim is to 
lead your hedgehog home by rolling the 
fuzzy ball and to be the first one to get there! 
Which one of you will be named the fastest 
hedgehog in the forest?

Playing the Game
Take 18 forest goodies and place them in the 
middle of the table the velcro (no picture!) side up.
The youngest player takes the fuzzy ball — they 
will be the first player and then you’ll take turns 
proceeding clockwise.
 Roll the ball! 
 Roll the ball any way you like, aiming 
to stick some forest goodies to it.
 Collect the tokens! 
 Collect all the forest goodies stuck to
the ball or flipped over (the picture side up). 
Place them in front of you.

End of the Game
Some clarifications: 
 You must always follow the path and you can’t skip 
any clearings without using the goodies. 
 You may use some or all collected goodies, but you 
may use each of them only once, and you can’t keep 
the leftover goodies for the next turns. 
You don’t move, if: 
 You’ve collected 5 forest goodies or more; 
 You haven’t collected any goodies at all or you don’t 
have the goodies you need to move along the path to 
an adjacent clearing. 
Place goodies in the pile!
Place all collected forest goodies in one pile the 
picture side up.
Check the pile!
4 or more forest goodies of the same kind in the pile? 
Place all collected forest goodies from the pile back 
on the table the velcro side up and shuffle them.
Move your hedgehog! 
For each collected apple/leaf/mushroom, you can move your hedgehog to an adjacent clearing 
with this goodie:
Move the fox! (Cooperative mode only)
After moving the hedgehog, move the fox 2 steps forward 
(the fox always follows the hedgehog using the shortest route).
If your hedgehog safely reaches the house, 
your team wins. You lose, if the fox catches 
the hedgehog first (stops at the same spot 
where your hedgehog is standing). 
As soon as someone has entered the 
house, the players who haven’t rolled the ball 
during this round make one turn each (so 
that everyone has the same number of rolls 
throughout the game). All players who have 
reached the house win the game.
