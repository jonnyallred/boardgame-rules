the active player, starting with the first player.

- During the game, players can handle their dice to look at 
them as long as they return the dice to their Hero Inventory in 
their original position.
- Forging a die face is the only way to modify a die. Players 
cannot rearrange the faces on their dice or put a pre-
viously-removed face back on a die.
- A Hero pawn remains in its location until it is either 
ousted or voluntarily moved to another portal.
- A player can perform any Heroic Feat, including
