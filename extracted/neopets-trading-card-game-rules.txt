Gather your favourite Neopets and challenge a friend to contests in strength, agility,
magic, and intelligence. When you win a contest, you get to put an Item or Equipment
card in your bank. The first one to put 21 points of Items and Equipment in the bank wins!
Sometimes, a little food, a potion, or even a new
paintjob right before a contest is all the edge
your Neopet needs to win.

after round.
